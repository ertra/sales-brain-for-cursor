# Sales Brain Templates

Templates are stored in the `templates/` directory. Use these as the basis for all new files:

- `templates/pain-points-template.md`
- `templates/value-proposition-template.md`
- `templates/use-case-template.md`
- `templates/competitor-template.md`
- `templates/objection-template.md`
- `templates/case-study-template.md`
- `templates/sales-play-template.md`

## company.md Template

```markdown
# {Company Name}

## Overview
{2-3 sentence company description}

## Industry
{Primary industry}

## Founded
{Year}

## Headquarters
{Location}

## Company Size
{Employee range}

## Mission/Vision
{Statement}

## Target Market
{Customer segments}

## Key Differentiators
{Unique selling points}

## Website
{URL}

## Additional Notes
{Other relevant info}
```

## products/{product-name}.md Template

```markdown
# {Product Name}

## Overview
{Product description}

## Main Features

- Feature 2
- Feature 3

## Problem Solved
{Customer problem addressed}

## Value Proposition
{Unique value delivered}

## Target Customers
{Ideal customer profile}

## Competition
| Competitor | Key Difference |
|------------|----------------|
| Competitor 1 | Difference |
| Competitor 2 | Difference |

## Pricing Model
{If available}

## Additional Notes
{Other info}
```

## personas/{persona-name}.md Template

```markdown
# {Persona Name}

## Role/Title
{Job title and role}

## Responsibilities
{Key responsibilities and duties}

## Company Profile
{Type of company they work for, size, industry - generic description}

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Problem 1
- Problem 2
- Problem 3

## Goals & Objectives
{What they're trying to achieve}

## How Our Products Solve Their Problems
{How the company's products address this persona's specific problems}

## Buying Process
{How they typically make purchasing decisions}

## Communication Preferences
{How they prefer to be reached, what messaging resonates}

## Additional Notes
{Other relevant info}
```
