## Executive Summary
3M uses Salesloft as a one-stop-shop platform to close deals 2.5x faster and meet a variety of revenue needs across sales operations and execution. The platform delivers consistency, visibility, and speed from pipeline to close.

## The Customer

- **Industry**: Manufacturing; diversified technology and operations
- **Size**: Enterprise; global footprint
- **Location**: Headquarters St. Paul, MN; operations worldwide
- **Business Model**: B2B; multiple divisions and sales motions

## The Challenge

### Situation Before
- Need to close deals faster and with more consistency across teams
- Multiple sales and operational needs; desire for one platform instead of point tools
- Pipeline, deal visibility, and execution consistency were priorities

### Key Pain Points
- Time to close too long
- Variety of solutions needed (engagement, visibility, consistency) without tool sprawl
- Alignment across US & Canada Sales Operations and enterprise customer operations

### Business Impact
- Slower deal cycles; potential revenue left on the table
- Operational complexity from multiple tools or inconsistent processes

## The Solution

### Why They Chose Salesloft
- One platform for a variety of revenue and sales operations needs
- Ability to drive faster close and consistent execution
- Enterprise-ready; fit for US & Canada and enterprise customer operations

### Products Implemented
| Product / Capability | Use Case |
