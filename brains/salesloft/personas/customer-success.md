# Customer Success

## Role/Title
Customer Success Managers (CSMs), customer success teams. Own delivering value, earning loyalty, and long-term growth. Reduce churn and cultivate trust throughout the full customer experience.

## Responsibilities
- Build customer relationships; increase satisfaction; keep customers connected for long-term growth
- Create champions with consistent engagement; increase loyalty and adoption with relevant, personalized content
- Increase revenue by spotting growth opportunities; align actions to deepen relationships and create new opportunities
- Combat churn using real-time insights; drive timely mitigation; close more renewals with automations that scale

## Company Profile
B2B organizations with recurring revenue and expansion/renewal motion. CS teams need visibility into health, engagement, and risk; often share goals with sales on retention and expansion. Use CRM and sometimes separate CS tools; benefit from one platform for cadences, conversations, and deals.

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Churn anticipated too late; manual touchpoints don’t scale
- Inconsistent engagement; generic content; hard to personalize at scale
- Lack of visibility into growth opportunities and next best actions
- Renewals and follow-ups manual; no single view of customer health and risks

## Goals & Objectives
- Deliver value, earn loyalty; reduce churn and cultivate trust
- Create champions with consistent engagement; delight customers with relevant, personalized content (generative AI by individual, any channel, from workflow)
- Increase revenue by spotting growth opportunities; account prioritization agents analyze buyer signals to understand desires and anticipate next moves
- Combat churn with real-time insights and data/AI agents; drive timely mitigation; close more renewals with automations for critical touchpoints

## How Our Products Solve Their Problems
- **Cadence** — Delight new customers and grow recurring revenue with best-practice cadence frameworks; multi-touch campaigns personalized by generative AI for onboarding, adoption, renewals, expansions
- **Conversations** — Build trust using conversation data; align team on real-time customer insights and feedback; accelerate follow-ups with AI-generated action items and summaries
- **Forecast** — Accurately forecast renewals, churn, expansions with built-in AI analysis; track performance to plan; take timely action
- **Deals** — Visibility into new deals, renewals, upgrades, partner resells; agent-generated deal summaries; assess and act on flagged risks in workflow
- **AI-driven account prioritization** — Align actions to deepen relationships and create new opportunities

## Buying Process
Customer Success leaders or RevOps often lead. Evaluation focuses on churn reduction, renewal predictability, scalability of touchpoints, and alignment with sales. Ease of use and scheduling (webinars, updates, follow-up meetings) matter to end users.

## Communication Preferences
"Deliver value. Earn loyalty." Reduce churn; create champions with consistent engagement; spot growth opportunities; combat churn with real-time insights. Customer cadences, AI-generated actions, full-funnel forecasting. "After using Salesloft, I could never imagine working without it. I can schedule webinars, software updates, and customer follow-up meetings with ease."
