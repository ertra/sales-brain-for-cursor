# Revenue Operations (RevOps)

## Role/Title
Revenue Operations leaders and practitioners (RevOps, Sales Operations, Business Transformation). Own alignment of sales and success on a unified platform, tech stack strategy, and predictable revenue outcomes.

## Responsibilities
- Align teams and grow revenue on a unified, efficient platform
- Harness and consolidate the full revenue stack; unify buying signals and activate data
- Untangle revenue complexities; show gaps and opportunities across the full funnel in one place
- Plan for future revenue growth; eliminate repetitive deal reviews, spot risks, execute confidently with forecast
- Create consistency with workflows; instill best practices, drive repeatable revenue
- Govern go-to-market initiatives with enterprise-grade controls and role-based provisioning

## Company Profile
B2B organizations from mid-market to enterprise; revenue teams that want one platform instead of point tools. Often have CRM, engagement tools, and multiple data sources; need consolidation and AI-driven workflows without heavy engineering (no-code CRM, low-code iPaaS, partner ecosystem).

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Scattered data and siloed teams; swivel-chairing between tools
- Repetitive deal reviews and manual forecasting; risks spotted too late
- Inconsistent workflows and lack of best-practice enforcement
- Need for governance and accountability without sacrificing productivity

## Goals & Objectives
- Optimize performance and produce results; build consistency with one platform
- Unify full revenue stack; power AI and agentic workflows with a comprehensive data foundation
- Effectively plan for future growth with accurate forecast and right actions
- Confidently govern GTM with granular role provisioning and enterprise controls

## How Our Products Solve Their Problems
- **Rhythm** — Deliver custom plays so reps prioritize actions most likely to convert; prioritization agent pulls real-time buyer signals
- **Forecast** — Accurately forecast across segments, pressure-test with AI, integrate forecasts into seller workflows
- **Deals** — Real-time customizable views; track opportunities, address risks, stay current on progression; keep teams aligned to hit targets
- **Cadence** — Repeatable plays and content in one place; customers, content, channels integrated; end swivel-chairing
- **Automation rules, cross-platform analytics, 180+ integrations** — Maximize time and reduce friction in the buying journey

## Buying Process
RevOps often leads or heavily influences platform selection. Evaluation focuses on consolidation ROI, time to value, governance, and alignment with existing CRM and tech stack. Proof from similar orgs (e.g. Wrike visibility quote) resonates.
