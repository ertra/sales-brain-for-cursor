# Sales Leader

## Role/Title
Sales leaders (VP Sales, Director of Sales, CRO). Own team performance, forecast accuracy, and consistent revenue growth. Lead decisively and grow consistently.

## Responsibilities
- Elevate team productivity while maximizing revenue predictability and business growth
- Coach teams to make more money; turn every seller into a top performer
- Formulate faster, repeatable success; scale winning behaviors and improve win rates
- Increase predictability with superior deal visibility; track progress to goals and plan for growth

## Company Profile
B2B sales organizations from mid-market to enterprise. Teams need AI-led strategy, coaching at scale, and one place to see deals and forecast. Often already use CRM and some engagement tools; need visibility and fewer manual reviews.

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Pipeline review time too long; forecast not tight enough
- Coaching is reactive or inconsistent; hard to identify what actually drives wins
- Objections and stalled deals surface too late; reps don’t get the right actions in time
- Lack of holistic view of sales process and deal risk

## Goals & Objectives
- Lead decisively, grow consistently; confidently guide teams using AI-led strategy
- Coach team to make more money; identify actions that translate to revenue impact and replicate what wins
- Increase predictability with superior deal visibility; utilize AI Forecast to plan and take control
- Gain holistic view of sales process; call out risks and opportunities to coach struggling reps

## How Our Products Solve Their Problems
- **Coaching (Conversations + Command Center)** — Real-time insights to identify benchmarks and evaluate performance on revenue outcomes; uncover trends to replicate rep success; AI agents call out risks and opportunities to coach struggling reps
- **Deals** — Cut pipeline review time (e.g. "Deals tab cut our pipeline review time by half"); real-time customizable views; track opportunities, address risks, stay current on progression
- **Forecast** — Call your number with precision; manage risk and deliver to plan; combine real-time business insight and AI informed by buyer signal and interaction data
- **Command Center** — Holistic view of entire sales process; AI agents for deal risks and coaching prompts
- **Workflow automations** — Prompt the right actions in rep workflows

## Buying Process
Sales leaders are champions or key decision makers. Evaluation focuses on time back for selling and coaching, forecast accuracy, and visibility. Proof from peers (e.g. Beam VP Sales — pipeline review time cut by half) resonates.
