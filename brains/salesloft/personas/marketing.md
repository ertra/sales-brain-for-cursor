# Marketing

## Role/Title
Marketing leaders and practitioners (Director of Digital Experience, VP Marketing, demand gen, web, ABM). Own converting target audiences and hitting pipeline goals through personalized web experiences and alignment with sales.

## Responsibilities
- Convert target audiences; hit pipeline goals
- Create personalized web experiences across every touchpoint to engage and convert valuable website visitors into qualified sales opportunities
- Deliver real-time website personalization; deanonymize traffic and deliver targeted messaging
- Connect sales and marketing for seamless customer experiences; sync cadences to campaigns, speed up cycles, improve campaign performance

## Company Profile
B2B organizations with meaningful website traffic and pipeline goals. Marketing owns or shares ownership of lead quality, conversion, and handoff to sales. Often use marketing automation, CRM, and ABM; need to align with sales and prove pipeline impact.

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Unqualified opportunities; wasted time and spend; lack of intent-based targeting
- Anonymous website traffic; no real-time personalization or conversation
- Disconnect between marketing and sales; slow cycles and poor handoff
- Need to maximize marketing dollars and prove ROI

## Goals & Objectives
- Engage in-market buyers and maximize marketing dollars with AI and agentic-powered conversational marketing
- Deliver real-time website personalization; deanonymize traffic; engage visitors with relevant, personalized conversations (AI chat agents, 24/7, account-based and firmographic/demographic/behavioral data)
- Target the right potential customers at the right time; use AI agents and buyer engagement signals to drive conversions and identify who is ready to meet with sales
- Connect sales and marketing; sync cadences to campaigns; real-time notifications to loop in reps for live chat or meeting booking

## How Our Products Solve Their Problems
- **Drift (AI chat agents, buyer intent, website personalization)** — Deanonymize traffic; personalized conversations; 6sense, ZoomInfo, Crunchbase; outcome-driven chat (e.g. book meetings, qualify leads); ABM for high-value target accounts
- **Buyer signals and prioritization** — Account engagement summaries, top pages, engagement scores; AI prioritization agent for intent-based accounts
- **Multi-channel engagement** — Route prospects to right cadences or tailored web experience or live chat; frictionless experiences across channels
- **Sales alignment** — Sync sales cadences to marketing campaigns; real-time notifications for reps; buyers can book meetings anytime

## Buying Process
Marketing often leads or co-leads with sales. Evaluation focuses on pipeline quality, conversion rates, sales alignment, and ROI. Proof: pipeline goals hit, alignment with sales, conversion and engagement metrics. Customer stories (e.g. Okta, Drift) resonate.

## Communication Preferences
"Convert target audiences. Hit pipeline goals." Create personalized web experiences; engage in-market buyers with AI and conversational marketing. Deliver real-time personalization; target the right accounts; connect sales and marketing. "We've only experienced an upside with Drift. It's helping us hit our pipeline goals and align our sales and marketing teams."
