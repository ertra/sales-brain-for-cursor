# Cadence

## Company
Salesloft

## Overview
Cadence is Salesloft's sales automation and cadence software for revenue-generating teams. It takes the guesswork out of pipeline generation, lead nurture, and renewals by orchestrating every call, email, and meeting. AI helps prioritize the right buyers and improve every interaction. Rated 4.5 on G2.

## Main Features
- **Account Agents** — Comprehensive account research, buyer lead identification, personalized email drafts in seconds
- **Cadence Focus Zone** — AI-powered prioritization; sort steps by channel, time zone, account
- **Dialer/Messenger** — Call from platform, cell, or hard line; capture details automatically; optional third-party dialer; text via messenger
- **Calendaring** — Salesloft Meetings; recipients schedule from emails
- **Templates/Snippets** — Reusable messaging; snippets for industry details, common answers
- Multi-channel cadences (4.7x engagement improvement); A/B testing; activity capture; CRM sync; Buyer Identification Agent
