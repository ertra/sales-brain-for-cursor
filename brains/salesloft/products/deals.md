# Deals

## Company
Salesloft

## Overview
Salesloft Deals is deal and opportunity management software that gives teams clarity and focus to win. AI agents help understand where every deal stands, what's at risk, and what to do to speed up deal cycles and close more business. Integrates with CRM for a complete view. Rated 4.5 on G2.

## Main Features
- **Close Focus Zone** — Prioritizes deals most likely to close; account planning agents rank by close likelihood
- **Auto Buying Group Capture** — Automatically creates CRM records for every email/meeting recipient; associates with deal so buying group is visible
- **Deal Qualification Frameworks** — Sales Methodology Extraction AI Agent pulls MEDDPICC, BANT, SPIN (etc.) from conversations
- **Deal Analytics Overview Dashboard** — Pipeline view with drill-downs; AI-recommended next steps; deal gap visibility
- **Deals Flow chart** — Conversion rates, idle deals, lost committed opportunities; refine forecasts and strategy

## Problem Solved
- Misaligned teams — Deal Summary AI Agent, shared dashboards, task assignment, manager coaching to close
- Time wasted on dead ends — AI spots winnable deals and risks; stalled-deal analysis and recommended actions in Rhythm
- Incomplete, disconnected data — Real-time view of every opportunity; automatic CRM sync; AI Forecast agent for pipeline prediction

## Value Proposition
Work smarter, close faster, win more: one place to manage opportunities, see what needs attention, and advance deals. AI-driven prioritization and next steps inside the seller workflow.

## Target Customers
Account executives, sales managers, revenue operations; teams with many opportunities and need for forecast visibility.
