# Rhythm

## Company
Salesloft

## Overview
Rhythm is Salesloft's sales workflow automation and process management software. It gives revenue teams clarity to act first on what matters most. AI prioritizes the most impactful seller actions so teams work smarter and win more deals. Rated 4.5 on G2.

## Main Features
- **Priority Accounts Agent** — Ranks accounts by engagement and buying intent; account-based focus
- **Signals** — Unifies buying signals from platform, tech stack, proprietary data, custom/BI sources
- **Plays** — Automations triggered by events/signals (e.g. meeting prep, share agenda, follow-up email); triggers include buyer intent, website engagement, meeting follow-up, product usage, health score
- **Focus Zones** — Prioritized list by area (e.g. Cadence Focus Zone for pipeline, Close Focus Zone for deals)

## Problem Solved
- Knowing where to focus team attention
- Disconnected buyer and seller experiences
- Time lost on non-selling activities and tool-switching

## Value Proposition
Act with certainty: Rhythm brings buyer signals into one place; Prioritizer AI Agent turns them into a prioritized workflow per seller. Role-specific actions from enriched buyer signals (website activity, content views, upsell/cross-sell). CRM and sales-tech integration with automated activity updates. Cited outcomes: 20% reduction in cycle time, 25% increase in close rates, 39% decrease in activities to schedule a meeting.

## Target Customers
Sales reps, revenue operations, sales leaders; teams running account-based and pipeline motions.
