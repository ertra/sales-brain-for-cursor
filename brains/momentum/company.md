# Momentum

## Overview
Momentum is the **AI Revenue Orchestration Platform** that captures unstructured data from every customer interaction, routes insights at speed, and automates execution across revenue systems. Tagline: *Your Revenue Engine Supercharged. First Call to Renewals.* The platform turns customer interactions into structured GTM data—fueling real-time execution, CRM automation, and insights that drive predictable revenue. Momentum has entered into a definitive agreement to be acquired by [Salesforce](https://www.salesforce.com/news/stories/salesforce-signs-definitive-agreement-to-acquire-momentum/).

## Key Products & Agents
- **Deal Execution Agent** — Write to Salesforce fields, save notes, save hours of manual work; MEDDPIC Autopilot, AI Summaries, suggested tasks/emails.
- **Customer Retention Agent** — Capture churn, sentiment, feedback; churn risk signals, sentiment analysis, AI notes + cases.
- **Coaching Agent** — Automated AI sales coaching; AI coaching score, team performance reports, competency review.
- **AI CRO** — Executive insights across accounts, reps, pipeline; CRO briefs, pipeline forecasting, team analysis.
- **SmartClips** — Key call moments into short, shareable video snippets.
- **Deep Research** — Analyzes deal data in conversations and CRM across the customer journey.
- **Integrations** — Team tools, CRM, Slack (Microsoft Teams coming), Snowflake, etc.

## Key Differentiators
- **AI-native Revenue Orchestration** — Extract, structure, and move GTM data automatically; CRM updated in real time (text, dropdowns).
- **Right message, right person, right time** — Slack as command center; deal rooms, key moments/risks/updates routed to Sales, CS, Leadership.
- **Zero manual effort** — Workflows run in background; follow-ups, stage moves by exit criteria; no guesswork, no manual clean-up.
- **Structured data from every interaction** — Cleaner pipeline, better reporting, smarter AI agents with context.
- **Security** — SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, CCPA, SSO, Zero-Retention Data Policy; [Trust Center](https://www.momentum.io/trust-center).
