# Customer Success Leader

## Company
Momentum

## Role/Title
Customer Success Leader, VP/Director Customer Success, Head of CS. Owns retention, expansion, and customer health; aligns CS with sales and product.

## Responsibilities
- Retain and grow accounts; reduce churn and drive NRR/expansion
- Flag at-risk accounts early; streamline sales-to-CS handoff with full context
- Give team accurate data and time back; keep C-suite updated on retention and sentiment

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Churn and risk discovered too late; no real-time view of sentiment from calls and tickets
- Sales-to-CS handoff incomplete; CS rediscovers account from scratch
- Manual notes and cases; CRM and workflow out of sync
- C-suite visibility into retention and risk is reactive, not proactive

## Goals & Objectives
- Flag churn risk in real time; act before renewal
- Smooth handoff with Account Rooms and better CRM data; seamless customer experience
- Capture customer success notes and cases automatically; log in Salesforce and sync to workflow
- Alerts for impactful customers (churn risk, competitor mentions, sentiment) to leadership

## How Momentum Solves Their Problems
- **Customer Retention Agent** — Churn risk signals from interactions; instant Slack notifications; auto-written cases and notes in Salesforce; sentiment analysis across calls, tickets, emails
- **Deal Execution Agent** — Automated notes and field updates; win recap and context so CS has decision maker, close reason, product concerns
- **Slack/Account Rooms** — Sales-to-CS handoff with full context; key moments and risks routed to CS
- **AI CRO** — C-suite alerts for churn risk, competitor mentions, customer sentiment

## Buying Process
Often evaluates with RevOps and sales leadership; CRO may sponsor. Cares about retention outcomes, handoff quality, and time back for CSMs. Proof: “Momentum flags risks in real time, enabling us to save critical accounts” (Demandbase).

## Communication Preferences
“Flag churn risk in real time,” “streamline sales-to-CS handoff,” “capture accurate data and give your team time back.” Proof from VP RevOps and CS leaders (Demandbase, Ramp).
