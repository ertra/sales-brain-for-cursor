# Momentum Platform (AI Revenue Orchestration)

## Company
Momentum

## Overview
Momentum is the AI-native Revenue Orchestration Platform that captures unstructured data from every customer interaction, structures it, and automates execution across revenue systems. Core promise: extract, structure, and move GTM data automatically; right message to the right person at the right time; automate processes with zero manual effort.
