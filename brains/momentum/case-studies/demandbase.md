## Executive Summary
Demandbase uses Momentum on a daily and hourly basis: every call is captured and analyzed to pull information that drives the business forward. Momentum summarizes across all calls without analysts having to spend hours curating data—and flags churn risk in real time so the team can save critical accounts.

## The Challenge
- Need to identify at-risk accounts and act before renewal
- Analysts and RevOps spending hours curating data from calls; need automation and scale
- Turning every customer interaction into actionable insight

## The Solution
- Customer Retention Agent: churn risk signals; instant Slack notifications; proactive action
- Deal Execution + AI: summarize across all calls; extract insights; update CRM
- Slack and workflow: right people notified so they can act same day

## The Results
- **Save critical accounts**: "Momentum flags risks in real time, enabling us to save critical accounts effortlessly."
- **Insight at scale**: "We use Momentum on a daily and hourly basis. Every call is recorded and scraped by Momentum to pull information out that drives our business forward. It summarizes across all the calls without our analysts having to spend hours curating the data."
- Real-time risk visibility; time back for analysts and CS
