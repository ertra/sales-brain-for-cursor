## Company
LAUTIE (Lautie EDC) is the **premium metal fidget and EDC brand** created by craftsman Yangman and designer Niko. In-house manufacturing (“Lautie factory”), metal construction, and strict QC. Tagline: **Stay Cool & Be Different.** Product range: fidget spinners, sliders, rings, pendants, EDC and DIY accessories. D2C via lautie.com; worldwide shipping; free shipping on orders over $50 USD.
