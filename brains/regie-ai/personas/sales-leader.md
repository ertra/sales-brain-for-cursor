# Sales Leader

## Overview
Sales leaders at Regie.ai target pipeline growth, rep productivity, and predictable execution without adding headcount.

- **Role/Title**: VP Sales, Head of Sales, Director of Sales
- **Core Responsibilities**: Pipeline creation, quota attainment, team performance
- **Typical Company Context**: B2B teams using SEP + dialer tooling

## Pains, Drivers, and Priorities
- Scaling outreach while maintaining quality
