# Use Case: Scale Pipeline With AI SEP

## Overview
Teams adopt Regie.ai to increase outbound pipeline while reducing manual prospecting burden on SDRs and AEs.

## Context
- **Target Company Profile**: B2B SaaS/enterprise teams with outbound motion
- **Primary Persona**: Sales Leader
- **Secondary Stakeholders**: SDR leadership, RevOps
- **Trigger Event**: Pipeline targets increase without approved headcount growth

## Challenge -> Solution -> Outcome
- **Current State**: Disconnected outbound tools and inconsistent personalization
- **Desired State**: Unified engagement flow with better quality and velocity
- **Solution Overview**: RegieOne coordinates agents and reps for signal-driven prospecting and execution

## Implementation Path
1. Baseline current outbound metrics and workflow.
2. Configure target ICP, channels, and guardrails.
3. Launch pilot with one segment/team.
4. Review results and expand rollout.
