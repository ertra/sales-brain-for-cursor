# Sales Play: Competitive Displacement (Outreach/Salesloft)

## Play Overview
| | |
|--|--|
| **Play Name** | Competitive Displacement (Outreach/Salesloft) |
| **Target Persona** | Sales Leader |
| **Target Company** | Mid-market to enterprise B2B teams |
| **Products** | RegieOne, Prospecting Agents, AI Dialer |
| **Trigger Event** | Incumbent SEP dissatisfaction or consolidation push |
| **Play Type** | Competitive |

## Entry Criteria
- **Trigger Events**: Renewal period, low reply rates, pipeline shortfall
- **Ideal Signals**: Tool sprawl, high manual prospecting burden
- **Qualification Criteria**: Outbound motion with measurable KPIs
- **Disqualify If**: No change initiative or no pilot owner

## Execution Sequence
1. **Research & Prep**: Map current stack and pain points.
2. **Initial Outreach**: Lead with AI-native differentiation and business outcomes.
3. **Follow-Up Sequence**: Share proof points and pilot path.
4. **Discovery**: Quantify manual burden and conversion gaps.
5. **Demo/Proposal**: Show workflow consolidation and KPI tracking.

## Messaging Assets
- **15s Talk Track**: Move from legacy SEP workflows to AI-native execution.
- **30s Talk Track**: Regie.ai combines agents and reps to improve quality outreach and pipeline velocity.
- **2min Talk Track**: Replace fragmented tooling with one AI-native engagement flow, prove value through a controlled pilot.
- **Primary CTA**: Launch a 30-60 day pilot on one segment.

## Objection Handling
| Common Objection | Response |
|------------------|----------|
| "We already use Outreach/Salesloft" | Differentiate on AI-native orchestration and consolidation outcomes. |
| "This seems like another tool" | Position as reducing tool sprawl, not adding complexity. |
| "Not the right time" | Offer scoped pilot with clear success metrics. |
