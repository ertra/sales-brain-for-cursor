## Overview
Renaissance adopted Outreach to replace manual, low-insight sales processes with a data-driven execution model. The story highlights higher conversion, strong seller adoption, and broader usage beyond sales.

## Challenge -> Solution -> Results
- **Challenge**: Manual CRM updates and limited insight into which activities drove pipeline impact.
- **Solution**: Rolled out Outreach as execution platform to provide visibility, guidance, and workflow optimization.
- **Results**: 93% increase in meeting-to-opportunity conversion rate and reported 87% seller adoption in early rollout.
