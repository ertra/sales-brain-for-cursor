## Overview
Worldpay used Outreach to improve pipeline generation and conversion through more strategic, data-informed sales development workflows. The case emphasizes measurable pipeline growth and stronger performance insight for reps.

## Challenge -> Solution -> Results
- **Challenge**: Needed better conversion and stronger execution quality in sales development processes.
- **Solution**: Implemented strategic sequence workflows with performance visibility in Outreach.
- **Results**: Added close to $100M in pipeline, improved conversion rates by 10%, increased email open rates by 25%, and response rates by 15%.
