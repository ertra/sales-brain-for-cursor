## Overview
Snowflake used Outreach to operationalize account-based sales motions and improve rep consistency as it moved upmarket. The platform helped reps prioritize accounts, align cross-functional messaging, and execute repeatable workflows.

## Challenge -> Solution -> Results
- **Challenge**: Enterprise ABM motion required strong process discipline, account prioritization, and consistent cross-team execution.
- **Solution**: Outreach became core execution layer for SDRs/AEs with sequences, triggers, and performance insight loops.
- **Results**: 15% more reps meeting quarterly quota (story headline) and reported 20% average prospect reply rate.
