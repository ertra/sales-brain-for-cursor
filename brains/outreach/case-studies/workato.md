# Case Study: Workato

## Overview
Workato used Outreach to balance personalization with scale as it entered new markets and targeted new personas. The team operationalized A/B testing, sentiment-driven messaging refinement, and pipeline-health monitoring to drive expansion outcomes.

## Challenge -> Solution -> Results
- **Challenge**: Rapid growth required new-market messaging and repeatable outbound process without losing personalization quality.
- **Solution**: Standardized workflows in Outreach, ran persona messaging tests, and used sentiment/insight data for optimization.
- **Results**: Expansion opportunities increased 68% quarter-over-quarter.
