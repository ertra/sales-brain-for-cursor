## Overview
BrightTALK switched to Outreach to improve visibility into rep-level sequence performance and operationalize a scalable sales-development process. The team used Outreach insights and sentiment analysis to improve coaching, alignment with marketing, and conversion outcomes.

## Challenge -> Solution -> Results
- **Challenge**: Existing tooling lacked rep-level visibility and actionable performance insight.
- **Solution**: Moved sales-development workflows to Outreach and used insights/sentiment analysis for optimization.
- **Results**: 25% increase in meetings booked and 66% increase in positive email reply rate.
