# Competitor: Clari

## Overview
Clari is a major enterprise competitor in forecasting, revenue orchestration, and RevOps-led pipeline management. It frequently appears in the same strategic buying conversations around predictability and revenue AI.

## Company Snapshot
- **Website**: https://www.clari.com/
- **Founded**: 2013 (Crunchbase, Clari about)
- **Headquarters**: Sunnyvale, California, US
- **Company Size**: ~500 employees (Crunchbase/TexAu). Manages $5T in revenue for 1,500+ customers.
- **Funding/Revenue**: ~$500M raised; $2.6B valuation (Jan 2022); ~$100M revenue 2022 (CB Insights). Note: Clari merged with Salesloft.
- **Key Customers**: Clari states it manages "$5T in revenue for 1,500+ customers."

## Product and Positioning
- **Key Products**: Capture, Inspect, Align, Copilot, Forecast, Analyze, Groove, Revenue AI Agents.
- **Tagline**: "Enterprise Revenue Orchestration."
- **Core Message**: See and act on every revenue signal; run every revenue workflow with AI + revenue context.
- **Target Market**: Enterprise RevOps, sales, finance, marketing, and post-sales organizations.

## Where They Win / Lose
- **Strengths**:
  - Strong enterprise and boardroom forecasting narrative.
  - Clear RevOps and forecast credibility positioning.
  - Heavy emphasis on data trust and revenue context.
  - Rich product coverage from prospecting to retention.
- **Weaknesses**:
  - Can be viewed as more enterprise-heavy for smaller/mid teams.
  - Evaluation may become forecast-centric if not expanded to end-to-end execution.
  - Buyers must validate operational adoption effort across broad module set.
  - Some marketing claims require verification in buyer-specific context.

## Battlecard
### When We Win
- Buyer needs strong execution consistency across frontline teams, not only forecast governance.
- Evaluation prioritizes adoption speed and measurable productivity lift.

### When They Win
- Deal is heavily led by enterprise RevOps/finance with forecast-first decision criteria.
- Buyer seeks Clari-specific ecosystem or existing internal standardization.

### Their Claims vs Reality
| Their Claim | Reality | Our Response |
|-------------|---------|--------------|
| "Only Revenue Orchestration Platform with Revenue Context" | Strong data/context narrative with broad scope | Compare both platforms against your most urgent workflow and KPI outcomes. |
| "398% ROI at Enterprise Scale" | Strong published ROI claim, context-specific | Validate expected ROI with your baseline metrics and change scope. |
