# Competitor: Gong

## Overview
Gong is a top competitor in revenue AI, conversation intelligence, and forecasting workflows. It is frequently compared against Outreach in enterprise GTM platform evaluations.

## Company Snapshot
- **Website**: https://www.gong.io/
- **Founded**: 2015 (Tracxn, Gong about)
- **Headquarters**: San Francisco, California, US (offices in Dublin, NYC, Singapore, Lehi UT)
- **Company Size**: 600+ employees (700+ by 2021 per Fortune; unicorn 2020)
- **Funding/Revenue**: $584M total funding; $7.25B valuation (Tracxn, Gong about)
- **Key Customers**: Gong states "5,000+ customers."

## Product and Positioning
- **Key Products**: Revenue Graph, Engage, Forecast, Agents, Collective.
- **Tagline**: "The #1 AI operating system for Revenue Teams."
- **Core Message**: Revenue AI OS with richest revenue graph, specialized AI agents, and apps for predictable growth.
- **Target Market**: Enterprise and high-growth revenue teams seeking AI-driven predictability/productivity.

## Where They Win / Lose
- **Strengths**:
  - Strong brand authority in conversation intelligence and revenue AI.
  - Clear data-centric "revenue graph" positioning.
  - Robust analyst and social proof narrative in messaging.
  - Enterprise-readiness and integration depth emphasis.
- **Weaknesses**:
  - Buyer may perceive complexity depending on scope and rollout maturity.
  - Evaluation can become intelligence-centric versus broader workflow execution needs.
  - Organizations seeking simpler, tightly guided workflow adoption may require narrower initial scope.
  - Outcome proof should be validated by segment and use case.

## Battlecard
### When We Win
- Buyer focuses on execution consistency across sales, RevOps, CS, and forecasting workflows.
- Decision criteria emphasize operational adoption and near-term KPI lift.

### When They Win
- Buyer prioritizes revenue-intelligence brand and data-graph narrative above workflow needs.
- Existing Gong champion or footprint strongly shapes criteria.

### Their Claims vs Reality
| Their Claim | Reality | Our Response |
|-------------|---------|--------------|
| "#1 AI operating system for Revenue Teams" | Strong brand and broad platform narrative | Compare against required workflows and measurable outcomes for your operating model. |
| "World's richest revenue graph" | Data-depth narrative is a core differentiator | Validate practical execution impact in your sales/RevOps/CS motions. |
