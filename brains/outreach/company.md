# Outreach

## Overview
Outreach is an AI revenue workflow platform for sales, marketing, revenue operations, and customer success teams. It positions itself as an end-to-end solution to help teams prospect, manage deals, forecast, coach, and expand customer accounts in one platform. The company emphasizes workflow orchestration, AI-generated insights, and forecast predictability to improve seller productivity and revenue outcomes.

## Company Snapshot
- **Industry**: Revenue technology / sales execution software
- **Founded**: 2014 (company states it started in early 2014)
- **Headquarters**: Seattle, Washington (Queen Anne waterfront; Built In Seattle, outreach.io/company/about)
- **Company Size**: ~1,000+ employees (1,023 total per Built In Seattle; 50,000+ sales reps at 3,300+ companies use Outreach)
- **Website**: https://www.outreach.io/

## Mission and Strategic Priorities
- **Mission/Vision**: Help customers change the world through revenue team execution software and AI-driven workflows.
- **Strategic Priorities**:
  - Increase seller productivity with AI workflows.
  - Improve deal execution and pipeline health.
  - Improve forecast confidence and predictability.
  - Support retention and expansion with coordinated account workflows.

## Target Market and ICP
- **Primary Segments**:
  - Sales organizations
  - Revenue operations teams
  - Marketing pipeline teams
  - Customer success and expansion teams
- **Buying Center Roles**:
  - Sales leadership
  - Sales managers
  - Sales development leaders
  - Revenue operations leaders
  - Customer success leadership
- **Best-Fit Signals**:
  - Teams seeking to consolidate fragmented sales tooling.
  - Organizations needing stronger forecast accuracy and deal inspection.
  - Revenue teams looking to standardize coaching and execution workflows.

## Differentiation
- **Key Differentiators**:
  - End-to-end AI revenue workflow platform across prospecting, forecasting, deal management, coaching, and retention.
  - Claimed measurable outcomes including productivity and forecasting improvements.
  - Role-based workflows for multiple go-to-market functions on one platform.
- **Competitive Context**:
  - Outreach positions directly against point solutions and against platforms such as Gong, Salesloft, and Clari (based on site navigation and comparison pages).
