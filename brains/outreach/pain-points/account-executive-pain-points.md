# Account Executive - Pain Points

## Overview
Account Executives need to keep opportunities progressing while coordinating multiple stakeholders. Their biggest pain is sustaining deal momentum without drowning in administrative work.

## Pain Point Breakdown

### 1. Deals stall due to weak stakeholder alignment
- **Description**: AEs struggle to keep all buyers aligned on scope, priorities, and next steps.
- **Business Impact**: Slower cycle times and reduced close rates.
- **Current Workaround**: Manual follow-up threads and ad hoc account plans.
- **Frequency**: Daily
- **Severity**: High
- **Mapped Product Solution**: Deal, Mutual action plans capabilities
- **Key Message**: Keep deals advancing with structured alignment workflows.

### 2. High admin load reduces selling time
- **Description**: Follow-up tasks, summaries, and CRM updates consume prime selling hours.
- **Business Impact**: Fewer high-quality customer interactions and lower opportunity throughput.
- **Current Workaround**: Copy-paste templates, personal task systems, delayed updates.
- **Frequency**: Daily
- **Severity**: High
- **Mapped Product Solution**: Meet, Call, Amplify
- **Key Message**: Convert conversation signals into immediate actions with less manual overhead.

### 3. Late visibility into deal risk and buying signals
- **Description**: AEs discover risk (silence, stakeholder gaps, weak momentum) too late.
- **Business Impact**: Last-minute deal recovery attempts and unpredictable outcomes.
- **Current Workaround**: Intuition-based prioritization and manager escalation.
- **Frequency**: Weekly
- **Severity**: High
- **Mapped Product Solution**: Deal, Forecast
- **Key Message**: Spot and act on risk earlier to protect close probability.
