# Customer Success Manager - Objection Handling

## Overview
CSM objections often focus on whether the platform is too sales-centric and whether it will actually help retention and expansion.

## Objection Plays

### 1. Fit: "This seems built for sales, not customer success"
- **Why They Say This**: Prior tools ignored post-sales workflows.
- **Acknowledge**: "That's a common concern in CS evaluations."
- **Respond**: "We should evaluate on renewal-risk visibility and account continuity outcomes, not sales labels."
- **Proof Point**: Outreach messaging includes retention and expansion workflows with early risk flags.
- **Follow-Up Question**: "What post-sales workflow gap hurts your renewals most today?"

### 2. Price/Budget: "CS budget is already tight"
- **Why They Say This**: CS teams compete for funding with sales priorities.
- **Acknowledge**: "Budget allocation across GTM is always a balancing act."
- **Respond**: "Let's tie this to churn-risk reduction and expansion opportunity capture."
- **Proof Point**: Customer listing notes +68% expansion opportunities in Workato story headline.
- **Follow-Up Question**: "Which retention or expansion metric can justify spend fastest?"

### 3. Timing: "We'll revisit after renewal season"
- **Why They Say This**: Team cannot absorb change during critical periods.
- **Acknowledge**: "Totally fair during a high-stakes renewal window."
- **Respond**: "Start with one account segment and early-risk use case to support, not disrupt, current process."
- **Proof Point**: Modular workflow rollout approach across packages.
- **Follow-Up Question**: "Which account tier could support a low-risk pilot?"

## Operator Guidance
### Questions to Ask Early
1. How early do you detect renewal risk today?
2. What context is missing in sales-to-CS handoff?
3. How are expansion opportunities currently identified?
4. Which CS metric is most urgent this quarter?

### Disqualification Criteria
- No retention/expansion KPI ownership.
- No cross-team collaboration between sales and CS.

### Talk Tracks
- **15s**: This is a lifecycle execution use case, not just a sales tool decision.
- **30s**: We can prove value on one renewal-risk segment with minimal process disruption.
- **2min**: CS objections are usually about relevance and change timing. By focusing on early risk detection and account continuity for one segment, teams can validate whether the platform drives retention outcomes before broader rollout.

### Next-Step CTA
- Select one renewal cohort for early-risk workflow pilot.
