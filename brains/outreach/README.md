## What This Is
This Sales Brain packages company, product, persona, pain, value proposition, use case, competitor, objection, case study, and sales play context for Outreach so teams can run consistent discovery, messaging, and execution.

## Outreach in One Sentence
Outreach positions itself as an AI Revenue Workflow Platform that helps revenue teams prospect, manage deals, forecast with confidence, coach performance, and improve retention/expansion in one workflow model.

1. **Workflow breadth** across engagement, deal, forecast, and AI-agent motions.
2. **Role-based coverage** for Sales, RevOps, Sales Managers, AEs, and Customer Success.
