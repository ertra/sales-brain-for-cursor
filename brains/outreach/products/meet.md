# Outreach Meet

## Overview
Outreach Meet is the conversation intelligence package (including Kaia capabilities) for real-time call assistance, post-meeting insights, and coaching support. It helps revenue teams capture and activate conversation signals across the platform.

## Problem and Outcome
- **Primary Problem**: Conversation insights are often trapped in calls and not consistently turned into coaching or next steps.
- **Business Outcome**: Better call quality, stronger coaching consistency, and faster post-meeting execution.
- **Who Benefits Most**: Account executives, sales managers, enablement teams, and customer-facing teams.

## Core Capabilities
- Real-time call recording and transcription.
- AI-powered smart meeting assist.
- Automated summaries and action items.
- Search, playlists, and clips for coaching and reuse.

## Value Proposition
Meet turns live customer conversations into actionable workflow signals, helping teams improve execution quality and scale coaching without adding manager overhead.

## ICP Fit and Disqualifiers
- **Best Fit**: Teams running frequent discovery/demo calls with a need to improve rep quality and coaching scale.
- **Disqualify If**:
  - Very low call volume and no structured coaching process.
  - No team ownership for call review or quality standards.
  - Strict recording constraints that block conversation capture.
