# Outreach Meet -> Sales Manager Value Proposition

## Overview
For sales managers, Outreach improves coaching quality and intervention speed by surfacing deal, pipeline, and conversation signals in one workflow. It helps managers spend less time collecting status and more time improving execution.

## Problem -> Solution -> Outcome
- **Problem**: Managers lack bandwidth to coach consistently and identify risk early.
- **Solution**: Conversation intelligence, AI summaries/action items, and deal/pipeline visibility in one platform.
- **Outcome**: Better rep consistency, earlier interventions, and stronger team performance.

## Value Drivers

- **Benefit**: Managers can review patterns faster and coach more reps effectively.
