# Target Companies

## Overview
Revenue.io sells to B2B organizations that run revenue and sales on **Salesforce**—from mid-market to large enterprises. Target segments are companies that want to orchestrate the full revenue process (calls, engagement, conversation intelligence, real-time guidance, forecasting) in one Salesforce-native platform. Referenced proof points: $300K increase in rep productivity, 120% quota attainment, 60% reduction in rep ramp time. Named customers include Nutanix, HPE, Fundera, NFI Industries; case studies available (e.g. [HPE](https://www.revenue.io/case-studies/hewlett-packard-enterprise)—400% more sales opportunities). The solutions hub segments by **role** (Sales Leaders, AEs, RevOps, SDR, Enablement, Customer Success, Marketing, Customer Support), **use case** (pipeline generation, sales engagement, quota attainment, AI coaching, ramp time, lead response, forecast intelligence), and **industry** (Technology, Financial Services, Insurance, Education, Telecommunications, Energy).

## Company Profiles

### Enterprise / global sales (e.g. Nutanix, HPE)
- **Industry**: Technology, IT, cross-industry; global sales into 150+ countries (per HPE example)
- **Size**: Large sales and GTM organizations; complex security and integration needs
- **Characteristics**: Need Salesforce-native dialer, conversation intelligence, real-time guidance, and forecast accuracy; care about compliance (HIPAA, TCPA, GDPR, SOC 2) and consolidating tools
- **Why They Buy**: More dials and connection rates; market-leading platform for inside sales; real-time visibility into deal health and rep execution; one platform for entire GTM
- **Common Challenges**: Tool sprawl, inconsistent rep performance, slow coaching, poor forecast accuracy, disconnected call and CRM data

### Mid-market / high-growth (e.g. Fundera, NFI Industries)
- **Industry**: B2B services, logistics, fintech, SaaS
- **Size**: Growing inside sales and SDR teams; often 20–200+ reps
- **Characteristics**: Need to scale meetings and pipeline without proportional headcount; want guided cadences, AI coaching, and instant lead response
- **Why They Buy**: 10x more monthly meetings (NFI); highly efficient sales workflows (Fundera); rep productivity gains (e.g. reps moving from $50K to seven figures); single platform for dialer, engagement, and intelligence
- **Common Challenges**: Ramp time for new reps, inconsistent follow-up, low connection rates, manual CRM updates, lack of in-call guidance

### Salesforce-centric revenue teams (any size)
- **Industry**: Any B2B where Salesforce is the system of record for revenue
- **Size**: From SMB to enterprise
- **Characteristics**: Want one Salesforce-native platform; care about automatic logging, clean objects, real-time visibility, and no data loss between tools
- **Why They Buy**: Consolidate dialer, engagement, conversation AI, real-time guidance, and forecasting; maximize data capture and context in Salesforce; eliminate jumping between tools
- **Common Challenges**: Disconnected tools, broken or incomplete CRM data, reps not following process, delayed or post-hoc coaching only
