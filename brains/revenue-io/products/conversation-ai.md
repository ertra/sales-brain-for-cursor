# Conversation AI (Conversation Intelligence)

## Company
Revenue.io

## Overview
AI-powered conversation intelligence that turns every call into revenue insights. Record, analyze, and coach every conversation with AI that identifies key moments, objections, and winning behaviors. Part of the Revenue.io platform with call recording, transcripts, and AI summaries.

## Main Features
- **Recording and transcripts**: Secure call recordings and generated transcripts so every call is searchable and actionable.
- **Conversation summaries**: AI call summaries with key insights and next steps to drive revenue impact.
- **Scorecards**: AI-driven scorecards that evaluate calls to improve rep performance and consistency.
- **Ask about a conversation**: Query anything about a conversation to uncover objections, decisions, and key insights.
- **Campaign and call tracking**: Track inbound/outbound calls and attribute to campaigns and outcomes.

## Problem Solved
Calls go unanalyzed; managers can’t scale coaching or spot winning behaviors and objections. Data lives in silos and doesn’t tie to deals.

## Value Proposition
"Record, analyze, and coach every conversation with AI that identifies key moments, objections, and winning behaviors." Conversation AI feeds real-time guidance (Moments) and rep coaching so insights turn into action.

## Target Customers
Sales leaders, enablement, and revenue operations who need scalable call analysis and coaching on Salesforce.

## Competition
| Competitor | Key Difference |
|------------|----------------|
| Gong / Chorus | Revenue.io is Salesforce-native with integrated dialer and real-time in-call guidance (Moments) |
| Wingman / Clari | Revenue.io bundles conversation AI with deal execution, forecasting, and rep coaching in one platform |

## Pricing Model
Part of Revenue.io platform; see https://www.revenue.io/pricing

## Additional Notes
- Call Intelligence capabilities include call tracking, routing, campaign tracking, recording, CTI for Salesforce, transcripts. Generative-AI capabilities include conversation summaries, scorecards, follow-up email, "Ask about a conversation."
