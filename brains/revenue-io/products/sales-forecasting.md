# Sales Forecasting

## Company
Revenue.io

## Overview
Improve forecast accuracy with real-time deal insights and AI-driven visibility into every opportunity in Salesforce. Revenue Forecast Analyzer and deal health/deal scorer identify pipeline risks and improve forecast accuracy with contextual AI.

## Main Features
- **Real-time deal insights**: Visibility into opportunity health, risk signals, and next steps across pipeline.
- **Revenue Forecast Analyzer**: AI-driven contextual analyzer that identifies pipeline risks and improves forecast accuracy.
- **Deal health / Deal scorer**: AI-powered deal scoring and health insights to reduce risk and forecast accurately.
- **Revenue Intelligence**: Comprehensive AI-powered revenue intelligence to monitor, measure, and optimize growth.

## Problem Solved
Forecasts are wrong; risks are found late and pipeline visibility is scattered across tools and spreadsheets.

## Value Proposition
"Improve forecast accuracy with real-time deal insights and AI-driven visibility into every opportunity in Salesforce." Forecasting is powered by conversation and activity data from the same platform.

## Target Customers
Sales leadership, RevOps, and finance who need accurate forecasts and pipeline risk visibility on Salesforce.
