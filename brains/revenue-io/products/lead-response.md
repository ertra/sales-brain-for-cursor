# Lead Response (Hot Leads)

## Overview
Connect every lead with call tracking, routing, and automation that turn inbound interest into immediate sales engagement. Hot Leads is the instant lead response engine that connects reps with buyers in real time so teams engage before competitors.

- **Hot Leads**: Instant lead response engine; connect reps with buyers in real time.
