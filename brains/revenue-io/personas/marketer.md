# Marketer

## Role/Title
VP/Director of Marketing, Demand Gen, Marketing Operations, Growth Marketing

## Responsibilities
- Connect campaigns to revenue; improve lead conversion and pipeline from marketing
- Align with Sales through full-funnel visibility; attribute leads and calls to campaigns
- Improve lead response time; route high-intent leads to the right rep quickly
- Prove marketing impact on pipeline and revenue

## Company Profile
B2B companies with marketing and sales alignment—mid-market to enterprise. Use Salesforce; care about attribution and speed-to-lead.

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Can’t tie campaigns to revenue; no clear attribution for calls and meetings
- Leads go cold when response is slow; sales and marketing work in silos
- No full-funnel visibility in Salesforce; marketing can’t see what happens after lead handoff
- Inbound calls and form fills not tracked or routed consistently

## Goals & Objectives
- Connect campaigns to revenue; attribute inbound calls and activities to pipeline
- Improve lead conversion and lead response time; engage hot leads before competitors
- Align with Sales through full-funnel visibility in Salesforce
- Track campaign performance and optimize based on revenue impact

## How Our Products Solve Their Problems
- **Lead Response (Hot Leads)**: Instant lead response engine; connect reps with buyers in real time; call tracking, routing, and automation for inbound interest
- **CallerDNA / Marketing Call Tracking**: Track inbound calls; tie campaign performance directly to revenue; attribute calls to campaigns
- **Sales Engagement + Guided Selling**: Cadences and automation so leads get fast, consistent follow-up
- **Revenue Platform**: Full-funnel visibility in Salesforce; marketing sees what happens after lead handoff
- Homepage: “Marketing: Turn high-intent leads into revenue by routing and responding instantly with full tracking.”

## Buying Process
- Often partner with Sales and RevOps; may co-own or influence lead routing and attribution tools
- Value attribution and speed-to-lead; care about Salesforce visibility and reporting
- May evaluate as part of broader revenue platform or lead management initiative

## Communication Preferences
- Outcomes: connect campaigns to revenue, improve conversion, faster lead response, full-funnel visibility
- Messaging: “connect campaigns to revenue,” “improve lead conversion,” “align with Sales through full-funnel visibility,” “routing and responding instantly”
- Attribution: call tracking, campaign attribution, pipeline impact
