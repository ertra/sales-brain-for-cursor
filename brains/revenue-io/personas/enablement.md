# Enablement

## Role/Title
Sales Enablement, VP/Director/Manager of Enablement, Sales Training, Learning & Development

## Responsibilities
- Scale coaching and reinforce sales methodology across the team
- Measure training impact and rep performance; tie to outcomes in Salesforce
- Reduce rep ramp time; improve time to quota for new hires
- Align content, playbooks, and best practices with how reps actually sell

## Company Profile
B2B companies with dedicated enablement—mid-market to enterprise. Care about consistent behavior, methodology, and measurable impact.

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Coaching doesn’t scale; managers can’t listen to every call
- Can’t measure impact of training; no clear link between enablement and pipeline/quota
- New reps ramp slowly; inconsistent application of methodology
- Insights are post-call only; reps repeat mistakes before feedback

## Goals & Objectives
- Scale coaching with AI-driven feedback and scorecards on every call
- Reinforce methodology and best practices in real time, not just in training
- Measure training impact directly in Salesforce (calls, deals, performance)
- Shorten ramp; 60% reduction in rep ramp time (site proof point)

## How Our Products Solve Their Problems
- **Rep Coaching**: AI-driven feedback, conversation scorecards, performance analytics; align reps to methodology and best practices
- **Moments**: Real-time in-call guidance so reps get coaching when it matters
- **Conversation AI**: Score every call; identify winning behaviors and coaching moments automatically
- **AI Coaching Feedback**: Automated coaching that aligns reps to sales methodology
- **Revenue Platform**: One place to see rep performance, call quality, and deal execution in Salesforce
- Use case: “AI Sales Coaching—Deliver personalized, real-time coaching that improves performance”; “Reduce Rep Ramp Time—Shorten onboarding, improve coaching, speed time to quota”

- Value measurable impact (ramp time, quota attainment, scorecard trends); want data in Salesforce
- Care about methodology alignment and real-time vs. post-call-only coaching
