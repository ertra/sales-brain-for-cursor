# Customer Success

## Role/Title
VP/Director of Customer Success, CSM, Customer Success Manager, Account Manager (post-sale)

## Responsibilities
- See customer health early; prevent churn and improve renewals
- Identify expansion and upsell opportunities; prove long-term value with data
- Run business reviews and success plans; share insights with stakeholders and execs
- Turn every conversation, email, and meeting into actionable insight

## Company Profile
B2B companies with Customer Success teams—mid-market to enterprise. Use Salesforce for accounts and activities; care about retention and expansion.

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Customer teams spend more time reacting to churn than identifying risk and expansion early
- No single view of account engagement, usage, and sentiment in real time
- Renewal risk and upsell potential surface too late; missing early warnings
- Can’t prove value or run data-backed business reviews without scattered data

## Goals & Objectives
- See every customer signal before it’s too late; move from reactive renewals to proactive engagement

- Get account health built on real customer activity; early warnings from real-time insights
