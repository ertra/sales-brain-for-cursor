## Key Differentiators

1. **Flexible data model** — Custom objects and relationships; build for PLG, deal flow, or any business. "Configure our CRM to fit our business needs, rather than the other way around." (Modal)
2. **Ask Attio** — Meeting prep, deal brief, daily brief from your actual CRM data. Real information, not AI invention; your permissions, enforced; approve, modify, or reject suggestions.
3. **Single source of truth** — Product, billing, and CRM data in one place; real-time sync; "it all just works" with everything downstream. (Flatfile)
4. **From zero to IPO** — Free to start, no credit card; 14-day Pro trial; import from Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, Excel, CSV. One CRM for sales, onboarding, CS, fundraising, recruiting.
