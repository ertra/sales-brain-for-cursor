# Attio

## Overview
Attio is an AI-native CRM built for go-to-market (GTM) teams. The platform is powerful, flexible, and data-driven, making it easy to build the exact CRM your business needs. Tagline: *"Ask more from CRM. Ask Attio."* — positioning Attio as the next-generation system of action for revenue teams.

## Target Market
- **Primary:** GTM teams (sales, revenue, RevOps)
- **Segments:** Scale-ups, SaaS startups, SMBs, and investors (deal-flow use case)
- **Notable customers (from site):** Bravado, Flatfile, Hightouch, Snackpass, Modal

## Key Differentiators
- **Adaptive data model** — Custom objects and attributes; CRM adapts to how your business works (scale-ups, SaaS, SMBs, investors).
- **AI-native** — "Ask Attio" (search and create), MCP/Developer Platform, AI for prospecting, lead scoring, and workflow triggers.
- **GTM at full throttle** — Workflows, automations, sequences, reporting, and data sync in one platform; sub-50ms latency at scale (e.g. 1M+ records).
- **Data enrichment & sync** — Email/calendar sync, Segment, data warehouses, billing, product data; real-time single source of truth.
- **Built for scale & trust** — GDPR, CCPA, ISO certified; import from Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, Excel, CSV.
