# Reporting

## Company
Attio

## Overview
Real-time reporting for go-to-market teams. Create bar, funnel, pie, line, and more. Attio turns millions of data points into insights for the whole team. "The reporting engine for go-to-market teams."

## Main Features
- **Real-time, flexible** — Access, visualize, and explore data as fast as you can think; analyze live data any way you like; customize with high flexibility.
- **Visualizations** — Line charts, bar graphs, geospatial mapping; combine into dashboards for the full story.
- **Data exploration** — Group your way (categorize, condense, manipulate in milliseconds); set better targets; historical data; drill down into report data.
- **Collaboration** — Build dashboards and reports with teammates in true real-time.
- **Use cases (from site)** — Sales (pipeline, stages, forecasts, win rates, time to close; ARR contribution, total pipeline); PLG (seat counts, workspaces, active/habit, accounts by plan, paid accounts, total ARR); leads (cumulative leads, channel distribution); RevOps (daily workspace creations, revenue per workspace, users per workspace, MRR, CAC).

## Problem Solved
GTM teams need reporting that keeps up with their data and questions. Attio reporting scales with data size and supports deep exploration without exporting to BI tools.

## Value Proposition
Find the insights you need quickly; build powerful dashboards; make better decisions with real-time, flexible reporting that works on top of Attio’s unified data model.

## Target Customers
Sales, Product-led Growth, Revenue Operations; any GTM team that needs pipeline, revenue, and product metrics in one place.

## Competition
| Competitor | Key Difference |
|------------|----------------|
| CRM standard reports | Attio: real-time, flexible grouping, historical, targets; dashboards; collaboration. |
| External BI (e.g. Looker, Tableau) | Attio: native to CRM data, no ETL for core GTM metrics; same permissions. |

## Pricing Model
- Free: up to 3 reports. Plus: up to 15. Pro: up to 100. Enterprise: 100+. Insight reports, funnel reports, segment by, targets, historical attributes, time comparisons. See [Pricing](https://attio.com/pricing).

## Additional Notes
- Reporting sits on top of Attio’s data model and sync (company, person, deal, custom objects, enrichment).
- 14-day free trial of Pro for full reporting capability.
