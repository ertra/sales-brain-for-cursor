# Ask Attio (AI)

## Company
Attio

## Overview
Ask Attio is Attio’s AI layer: "Ask more from CRM. Ask Attio." It delivers simply powerful customer intelligence — semantic search, meeting prep, deal updates, and daily briefs — powered by Universal Context and grounded in your CRM data with permissions enforced.

## Main Features
- **Semantic search** — Instant retrieval as data scales; grounded in emails, calls, product usage; understands patterns across customer signals; multilingual (e.g. EN, ES, PT, FR, DE, IT).
- **Universal Context** — Unified view of context (emails, calls, notes, Slack, etc.); "Granola notes. Slack threads. Your favorite tools, connected and searchable."
- **Meeting prep & recaps** — Prep for next meeting, recap last call; daily brief; deal brief; QBR prep; onboarding prep; recap call; update deal from call; suggest next steps.
- **AI actions** — Update deal/role/next step; product feedback extraction; objection handling; account research; follow-up email; shipped feature outreach; sales coach; product engagement summary.
- **Trust & control** — Real information, not AI invention (sourced from actual CRM); your permissions enforced; intelligent suggestions, human decisions (approve, modify, reject).

## Problem Solved
Reps waste time jumping between tools and copying context. Ask Attio centralizes, summarizes, and surfaces what’s needed for every meeting and deal so teams can give a great first impression and close faster.

## Value Proposition
"Before every meeting, Ask Attio centralizes, summarizes, and surfaces everything I need to know to give a great first impression or close the deal." — Ian Ahuja, Head of Sales (from site). Win faster when every call is perfectly prepped.

## Target Customers
Marketing, Success, Sales, Founders, Venture capital — any GTM role that needs fast, accurate context from CRM and connected tools.

## Competition
