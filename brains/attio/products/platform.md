# Attio Platform

## Company
Attio

## Overview
Attio is the AI CRM for GTM (go-to-market). One platform for workflows, AI, data, and reporting — "GTM at full throttle." Execute revenue strategy with precision: design workflows, deploy AI, integrate data, and build detailed reports with sub-50ms latency at scale.

## Main Features
- **Adaptive data model** — Custom objects and attributes; model reflects your business (scale-ups, SaaS, SMBs, investors).
- **Ask Attio (AI)** — Semantic search, meeting prep, deal updates, daily briefs; Universal Context; permissions enforced.
- **Workflows & automations** — Visual canvas, triggers, actions, AI blocks; sequences; integrations (Slack, Typeform, Mailchimp, Mixmax, webhooks).
- **Data & sync** — Email/calendar sync, data warehouses, reverse ETL, CDPs; enrichment (company, people); activity timelines.
- **Reporting** — Real-time reports; bar, funnel, pie, line, geospatial; group/targets/historical; dashboards; collaboration.
- **Call Intelligence** — (Pro+) call-related features.
- **Integrations** — API, webhooks, App SDK, MCP server; Gmail, Outlook, Segment, Slack, Outreach, Zapier; import from Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, Excel, CSV.

## Pricing Model
- **Free** — €0/user/mo. Real-time contact syncing, automatic enrichment, up to 3 seats; 100 seat credits/user/mo; 250 workspace credits/mo; 50k records; up to 3 workspace objects; 3 reports; 1 email account per user; 200 emails/mo.
- **Plus** — €36/mo or €29/mo (annual). Private lists, enhanced email, no seat limits; 500 seat credits; 1,500 workspace credits; 250k records; up to 5 objects; 15 reports; 1,000 emails/mo; 50 bulk emails.
- **Pro** (Popular) — €86/mo or €69/mo (annual). Call Intelligence, sequences, advanced permissions, priority support; 1,000 seat credits; 10,000 workspace credits; 1M records; up to 12 objects; 100 reports; unlimited emails; Ask Attio, AI agents; 2 email accounts per user; 14-day free trial.
- **Enterprise** — Custom. Unlimited objects, unlimited teams, advanced security & admin; 2,500 seat credits; custom workspace credits/records; 100+ reports; 3+ email accounts; migration service; SSO; payment by invoice.

*Workspace credit add-ons available (e.g. +5k/mo, +10k/mo, +25k/mo, +50k/mo).*
