# Automations & Workflows

## Company
Attio

## Overview
The future of automation, supercharged by AI. Build powerful workflows for your GTM team inside the CRM. "Automate. Iterate. Accelerate." Workflows turn GTM strategies into dynamic engines for revenue growth.

## Main Features
- **Visual canvas** — Create with an intuitive visual canvas; beautiful interface for complex processes; precise, step-by-step control.
- **Building blocks** — Triggers (Attio or external), actions (customizable), integration blocks; extend with formulas, calculations, new features.
- **AI in workflows** — Classify any data; generate in-depth summaries; research at scale (prospecting, lead qualification); automate tasks that need human judgment.
- **Integrations** — Outreach (sequences, contact interactions), Typeform (submissions → triggers), Slack (context, tasks, decisions), Mailchimp (audiences, subscribers), Mixmax (sequences), webhooks (data warehouses, CDPs, any tool).
- **Sequences** — Multiple senders, single sequence; capture high-intent leads; personalized nurture and onboarding flows; keep leads in the loop.
- **Templates** — Out-of-the-box templates: pass deals to CS, closed-won notification, Slack message on won deal, won/lost deal summary (AI), renewal date autofill, churn reminders, and more. [Automations library](https://attio.com/templates)

## Problem Solved
GTM teams run manual, repetitive processes and can’t automate complex or data-dependent flows. Attio automations give precise control and AI for unstructured data and judgment tasks.

## Value Proposition
No matter your GTM motion — product-led growth, product-led sales, sales-led growth, RevOps — Attio is built to drive it. Create better systems, integrate with your stack, and design powerful automations with AI.

## Target Customers
PLG teams (product + CRM context), PLS, sales-led teams, RevOps; small to enterprise.

## Competition
| Competitor | Key Difference |
|------------|----------------|
| Zapier/Make + CRM | Attio: workflows live inside CRM, same data model and permissions; no sync delay. |
| CRM-native automations (HubSpot, Salesforce) | Attio: visual canvas, AI blocks, flexible data model, modern UX. |

- "Build faster to go further" — get up and running with customizable templates.
