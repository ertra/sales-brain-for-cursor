# Attio → Marketing Value Proposition

## Summary
For marketing and demand teams, Attio means lead and campaign data in the same CRM as pipeline—one source of truth, real-time routing and triage, and full-funnel reporting without exports. Unify Segment, forms, and product data; automate MQL routing and nurture; segment by channel and source in real time. "83% faster lead triage."

## The Problem
Lead and campaign data disconnected from CRM; manual routing and triage; attribution and funnel reporting require exports and spreadsheets.

## The Solution
Attio's data model holds source, segment, and score; workflows trigger from Typeform, Segment, and webhooks to route and nurture. Same CRM so sales gets full context. Reporting segments by channel, source, and segment in real time. One funnel, one source of truth.

## Key Value Drivers

### 1. Lead and campaign data in the CRM
- **Benefit**: Source, segment, and product signals in the same record as contact and deal; routing and scoring on one dataset.
- **Proof Point**: Data model + sync (Segment, data warehouses); custom attributes; workflows from Typeform, webhooks.
- **Customer Quote**: (From data page) "Unify your data sources. Draw on all your business, customer and product data to supercharge your GTM team's efforts."

### 2. 83% faster lead triage
- **Benefit**: Automate routing by segment (e.g. enterprise vs SMB); trigger from form or product event; add to sequences; high-intent leads get to the right rep fast.
- **Proof Point**: Granola case study—"83% faster lead triage. How Granola turns product signals into revenue at scale."
- **Customer Quote**: "83% faster lead triage" — Granola case study (customers page)

### 3. Full-funnel reporting without exports
- **Benefit**: Segment by channel, source, and segment in Attio; real-time dashboards; prove what's working without merging exports.
- **Proof Point**: Attio Reporting—real-time, group your way, historical, targets; data model holds attribution-friendly attributes.
- **Customer Quote**: (From reporting page) "Powerful data exploration. No matter how large the data set, Attio makes it easy to deep dive into the details."
