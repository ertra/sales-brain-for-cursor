# Inbound → Marketer Value Proposition

## Summary
Apollo Inbound helps marketers convert more visitors and form leads into pipeline by identifying anonymous companies, enriching leads in real time, routing and scheduling instantly, and automating nurture—so hot leads never go cold and conversion and routing accuracy improve without a separate lead-routing stack.

## The Problem
Marketers lose pipeline when leads wait too long for first touch, forms are long and conversion suffers, and routing is manual or based on thin data. Disconnected tools for forms, enrichment, and routing mean no single view and slow optimization.

## The Solution
Apollo Inbound combines visitor tracking, form enrichment (auto-fill from email via Apollo Data), inbound router, meetings scheduler, and AI-built workflows—all on the same data layer sales uses. Marketers get real-time enrichment, instant routing, and analytics in one platform.

## Key Value Drivers

### 1. Don’t let hot leads go cold
- **Benefit**: Qualify and route leads in seconds; built-in scheduler so reps book meetings immediately; automate nurture and follow-up.
- **Proof Point**: Apollo case study: 300% routing accuracy improvement while boosting inbound conversions.
- **Customer Quote**: (Use site positioning) "Your next best customers are at your fingertips. Don't let them go cold." — Apollo Inbound positioning

### 2. Shorten forms and enrich in real time
- **Benefit**: One email can auto-fill key fields; 65+ data points for scoring and routing; higher quality leads and better assignment without long forms.
- **Proof Point**: Form enrichment and Apollo Data power qualification, routing, and follow-up in one flow.
- **Customer Quote**: "Convert inbound leads into B2B pipeline. Never let a hot lead go cold again." — Apollo Inbound

### 3. One platform for marketing and sales
- **Benefit**: Same data (Apollo Data) for inbound and outbound; shared analytics; no second enrichment or routing vendor.
- **Proof Point**: Emerge Market CMO tripled Apollo spend for 400% pipeline growth (magazine); single platform for visitor ID, enrichment, routing, nurture.
- **Customer Quote**: (Emerge Market) CMO drove 400% pipeline growth with Apollo—inbound and outbound on one platform.
