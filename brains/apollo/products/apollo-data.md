# Apollo Data

## Company
Apollo

## Overview
Apollo Data is Apollo’s B2B data network and sales intelligence layer: a large, verified business database (210M+ contacts, 30M+ companies) with high email accuracy, real-time verification, and multichannel reach. It powers outbound, inbound, enrichment, and deal execution across the Apollo platform.

## Main Features
- **Coverage**: 210M+ contacts, 30M+ companies; 72M emails verified monthly, 150M contacts refreshed monthly; 230M+ records processed monthly from partners
- **Verification**: 7-step email verification (bounce prediction, catch-all handling, real-time checks); real-time direct-dial and mobile verification; &lt;1% invalid direct phone numbers, 91% email accuracy
- **Depth**: 65+ data points per lead (firmographic, demographic, intent, employee trends); actionable lead intelligence in one view
- **Collection**: Data contributor network (2M+ sources), engagement suite (replies/bounces), public data crawling, vetted third-party providers
- **Privacy & security**: ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR (Processor and Controller), configurable privacy features

## Problem Solved
Teams struggle with incomplete, stale, or unverified B2B data—wasting time on research, wrong contacts, and low deliverability. Apollo Data provides one trusted, always-fresh source so GTM can target and reach the right buyers.

## Value Proposition
"B2B data that dominates." Unrivaled breadth and depth with industry-leading verification and privacy standards; benchmark wins vs. ZoomInfo, Clearbit, Lusha, Seamless on quality and enrichment.

## Target Customers
Sales and marketing teams of all sizes; companies that need accurate, scalable B2B contact and company data for prospecting, enrichment, and routing.

## Competition
| Competitor | Key Difference |
|------------|----------------|
| ZoomInfo | Apollo positions higher quality and enrichment; often cited as replacement with cost savings |
| Clearbit | Apollo emphasizes greater breadth and unified platform (data + engagement) |
| Lusha / Seamless | Apollo positions as full data + engagement platform vs. point solutions |

## Pricing Model
Credits-based; free tier available. Trial includes 50 credits, 5 mobile credits. Paid and Custom plans for teams and enterprises. See https://www.apollo.io/pricing

## Additional Notes
- Export credits consumed when syncing/exporting contacts outside Apollo (CSV, CRM, Person API). Integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Outreach, SalesLoft, Marketo, etc. API on Custom plans.
