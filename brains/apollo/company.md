# Apollo

## Overview
Apollo (Apollo.io) is an AI sales platform that helps B2B sales and marketing teams build pipeline, close deals faster, and simplify their tech stack. Founded in 2015, it is a leading data intelligence and sales engagement platform trusted by 40,000+ paying customers and used by over 1 million sales professionals. The platform combines one of the largest B2B data networks (210M+ contacts, 30M+ companies) with outbound, inbound, data enrichment, and deal execution in a single unified product.
