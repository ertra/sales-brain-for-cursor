# Case Study: Dry runZ (formerly PodiumX)

## Snapshot

| | |
|--|--|
| **Customer** | Dry runZ (formerly PodiumX) |
| **Industry** | Technology (exact vertical to be verified) |
| **Products Used** | AiSDR Platform (email campaigns, multi-segment) |
| **Primary Persona** | Founder / CEO (Yariv Erel, CEO and Founder) |
| **Use Case** | Cold email; first meeting speed; hands-off; small segments |

## Executive Summary

Dry runZ ran 6 email campaigns with AiSDR, each targeting a small segment. **First meeting booked 3 days after launch.** In 30 days they secured **8 meetings**, fully hands-off. Top campaign hit **9.41% response rate**; overall **5.7% total response rate**, **2.19% positive response rate**. Quote: “Everything is great. It’s exactly what I was hoping for.” — Yariv Erel, CEO and Founder. Strong proof for founders who need first meeting fast and don’t want to run outreach manually.

## The Customer

- **Company**: Dry runZ (formerly PodiumX).
- **Role**: Yariv Erel, CEO and Founder.
- **Context**: Small segments; multiple campaigns; founder-led evaluation.

## The Challenge

### Situation Before
- Need for pipeline and first meetings without full-time SDR or heavy manual work.
- Wanted results quickly and “hands-off.”

### Key Pain Points
- Speed to first meeting.
- Capacity to run multiple segments without manual effort.

## The Solution

### Why They Chose AiSDR
- Multiple campaigns (6), each targeting a small segment; AiSDR ran outreach and booking.
- Fully hands-off after setup; first meeting in 3 days.

### Capabilities Used
| Capability | Role |
|------------|------|
| Multi-campaign, multi-segment | 6 email campaigns; small segments |
| Meeting booking | 8 meetings in 30 days; first in 3 days |
| Hands-off operation | No ongoing manual send/follow-up |

## The Results

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| First meeting | 3 days since launch |
| Meetings in 30 days | 8 |
| Total response rate | 5.7% |
| Positive response rate | 2.19% |
| Best campaign response rate | 9.41% |
| Mode | Fully hands-off |

### Qualitative Outcomes
- “Everything is great. It’s exactly what I was hoping for.” — Yariv Erel.

## Sales Use

### Best For
- Founders and small teams that need first meeting in days, not weeks.
- Proof of “first meeting in 3 days” and “8 meetings in 30 days, fully hands-off.”
- Multiple segments; small, focused campaigns.

### Key Talking Points
1. “First meeting in 3 days since launch.”
2. “8 meetings in 30 days. Fully hands-off.”
3. “Top campaign: 9.41% response rate — CEO: ‘Exactly what I was hoping for.’”
4. “6 campaigns, small segments — no manual work.”

### Objections This Addresses
- “We need revenue yesterday.” → First meeting in 3 days.
- “We don’t have time to manage outreach.” → Fully hands-off.
- “Does it work for small teams?” → Founder quote; 8 meetings in 30 days.
