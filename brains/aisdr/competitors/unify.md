# Competitor: Unify

## Overview

Unify is positioned as serving **enterprise-scale operations** with deep CRM integration; comparisons note potential “complexity hurdles for smaller teams.” AiSDR’s differentiation is precision targeting and flexible terms for teams of any size, transparent pricing, and Live AI search. Unify is mentioned in AiSDR’s “Breeze vs Unify” blog and in comparison summaries as an enterprise-focused alternative.

## Company / Product (from public and AiSDR lens)

- **Positioning**: Enterprise-scale; deep CRM integration
- **Target**: Larger ops; “complexity hurdles for smaller teams” (per comparison summary)
- **Comparison**: AiSDR blog “Breeze vs Unify”

## Strengths (competitor)

- **Enterprise**: Built for scale and deep CRM integration
- **CRM**: Strong integration story for large, complex orgs

## Weaknesses (AiSDR’s likely angles)

- **Pricing / terms**: AiSDR leads with transparent, quarterly; Unify’s pricing and lock-in to be verified
