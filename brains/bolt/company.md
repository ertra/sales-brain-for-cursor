# Bolt

## Overview
Bolt (Bolt Technology OÜ) is the first European mobility super-app. It offers ride-hailing, food and grocery delivery, car-sharing, micromobility (scooters, e-bikes), and business travel in one app. Available in 600+ cities and 50+ countries, Bolt positions itself as "making cities for people, not cars" by providing better alternatives for every purpose a private car serves.

## Mission/Vision
"Make cities for people, not cars." Bolt’s shared mobility products reduce the need for private car use in cities and improve public transport connectivity. The app integrates ride-hailing, car-sharing, scooters, and delivery so users can choose the best option per trip.

## Services (Products)
- **Rides** — Request in seconds, ride in minutes; advance booking up to 90 days.
- **Bolt Food** — Food delivery.
- **Bolt Drive** — Car-sharing / high-quality car rental.
- **Bolt Market** — Grocery delivery.
- **Bolt Business** — Manage business travel for teams and clients.
- **Micromobility** — Scooters and e-bikes (2-wheel rental).
- **City solutions** — Sharing data and best practices with local authorities to make cities more connected and liveable.

## Target Market
- **Consumers**: Urban riders (no parking, fuel, or maintenance).
- **Partners**: Drivers, couriers, merchants, fleet owners (earn with Bolt).
- **Business**: Companies managing team and client travel (Bolt Business).
