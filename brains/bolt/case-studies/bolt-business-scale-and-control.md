## Executive Summary
Bolt Business presents a broad proof story rather than a single named customer case: 50,000+ businesses use the platform to manage business travel with one dashboard, central billing, and policy controls. The key value pattern is consistent: less manual expense work, better spend control, and easier rollout (no activation costs or minimum commitment).

## The Customer
- **Type**: Companies of any size across industries
- **Scale Signal**: "Powering global travel for 50,000+ businesses"
- **Stakeholders**: Travel, office, operations, finance, HR, admins

## The Challenge

### Situation Before
- Work travel spread across multiple apps and payment flows
- Manual reconciliation and fragmented reporting
- Limited policy enforcement and spend visibility

### Key Pain Points
- Manual expense admin and month-end reconciliation
- No single view of travel usage and costs
- Difficulty balancing budget control with employee convenience

## The Solution

### Why They Choose Bolt Business
- One platform for rides, meals, and additional mobility services
- Cost controls: custom spending limits and rules
- One consolidated invoice and reporting
- No activation costs or minimum commitment for adoption

### Products / Capabilities Involved
| Capability | Role |
|------------|------|
| Dashboard / central platform | Manage travel and policy in one place |
| Custom rules and limits | Control spend and usage |
| Automated reports + integrations | Reduce manual admin |
| Consolidated invoicing | Simplify finance operations |

## The Results

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Business adoption | 50,000+ businesses |
| Setup friction | No activation costs; no minimum commitment |
| Policy control | Custom limits and travel rules |
| Admin burden | Reduced via automated reports and one invoice |

### Qualitative Outcomes
- Better control and visibility for travel/finance stakeholders
- Faster rollout by team/region, then scale
- Cleaner governance for business travel and mobility spend

## Sales Use

### Best For
- B2B accounts with fragmented travel workflows
- Teams comparing Bolt Business vs. Uber for Business
- Prospects that need a low-risk pilot path

### Key Talking Points
1. "50,000+ businesses already run on Bolt Business."
2. "No activation cost, no minimum commitment - start small, scale fast."
3. "One platform and one invoice for control, visibility, and less admin."

### Objections This Addresses

- "We need proof this works at scale."
