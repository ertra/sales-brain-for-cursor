# Rides

## Company
Bolt

## Overview
Bolt Rides is the core ride-hailing service: fast, safe, affordable car rides 24/7 in 600+ cities. Users request a ride in seconds and get picked up in minutes, with options from budget to premium. Advance booking is available up to 90 days ahead for airport transfers, meetings, or any trip.

## Main Features
- **Request in seconds**: In-app ride request; millions of driver partners across 50+ countries, day or night
- **Ride types**: Quick trips, commutes, longer journeys; budget or premium options
- **Book in advance**: Schedule rides up to 90 days ahead for airports, meetings, punctuality
- **Safety**: Bolt Safety Team (500+ people); Emergency Assist, Women for women (female driver option), Ride Check (detects unexpected stops), share location, private number when calling via app, pickup codes, 24/7 support
- **Airports**: Transfers to/from 100+ airports worldwide
- **Mission**: Carbon net zero by 2040; "cities for people, not cars"

## Problem Solved
Getting from A to B without owning a car: no parking, fuel, or maintenance. Need for reliable, on-demand or pre-scheduled rides for personal or business use.
