# City Solutions

## Company
Bolt

## Overview
City Solutions is Bolt’s B2G (business-to-government) offering: sharing knowledge, real-time data, and best practices with local authorities to make cities more connected, liveable, and sustainable. Focus areas include managing micromobility, safety, sustainability, and multimodal transport (e-scooters, e-bikes, car-sharing, walking, charging docks).

## Main Features
- **Rethinking urban transport**: Goal to help cities find sustainable transport solutions using Bolt’s experience and data
- **Managing micromobility**: Insights from operating light vehicles in 200+ cities; guidance on street rental fees, authorisations for private operators, utilisation targets, data sharing, and enforcement
- **Tools**: E-scooters, e-bikes, car-sharing, multimodal travel, walking, charging docks — positioned as tools to reshape city transport
- **Safety**: Patented tandem prevention; cognitive reaction test; rider education (Safety Toolkit, Beginner Mode, local rules); responsible parking; AI pavement detection and parking guidance; accessibility (e.g. ambient noise for pedestrians/visually impaired)
- **Sustainability**: CarbonNeutral® for scooters/e-bikes; reduced CO2 and congestion; charging docks cutting battery-swap operations; recyclable materials for e-bikes
- **Data and integration**: Willingness to share data for compliance and planning; integration with European MaaS applications

## Problem Solved
Cities need to regulate and optimise shared mobility (congestion, emissions, safety, parking, equity). Bolt offers operator perspective, data, and policy input.

## Value Proposition
"Sustainable mobility for cities" / "Rethinking urban transport." Bolt helps cities improve transport and citizens’ lives through knowledge sharing, real-time data, and practical tools (micromobility, car-sharing, multimodal, safety, sustainability).

## Target Customers
Municipalities; transport and urban planning authorities; policymakers drafting or enforcing micromobility and mobility regulations.

## Pricing Model
B2G; contact Bolt for engagement. Revenue mechanisms for cities can include street rental fees for micromobility.
