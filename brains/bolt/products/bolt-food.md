# Bolt Food

## Company
Bolt

## Overview
Bolt Food is food delivery and takeout from local restaurants and stores. Users discover restaurants, order and pay in the app, and track delivery in real time. Available in the Bolt Food app (iOS/Android) or browser. Grocery delivery is also available via Bolt Market within the same app.

## Main Features
- **Discover**: Wide selection of restaurants and stores; "Recommended" section and ratings
- **Order and pay**: Few taps to place order; pay with cash, card, or Bolt balance
- **Track**: Real-time order tracking from store to door; notifications at every stage
- **Pickup**: "Pickup" option for takeaway (no delivery)
- **For couriers**: Earn per delivery; flexible schedule; bike, scooter, or car
- **For merchants**: Reach millions of users; business insights (sales, ratings, orders); Bolt handles delivery logistics

## Problem Solved
Getting favourite food (or groceries) delivered fast without multiple apps or hassle. For restaurants/stores: reaching more customers and increasing sales with delivery handled by Bolt.

## Value Proposition
"The food you love, delivered fast." One app for food and grocery; no subscription fee for couriers; merchants get delivery and insights.

## Target Customers
Consumers who want restaurant or grocery delivery; couriers looking for flexible earnings; restaurants and stores wanting to grow sales and reach.

## Pricing Model
Items priced in app; delivery fee and small order fee (below minimum) may apply. Total visible before confirming. No subscription for couriers; earnings paid weekly.
