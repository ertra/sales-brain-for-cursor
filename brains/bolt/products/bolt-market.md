# Bolt Market

## Company
Bolt

## Overview
Bolt Market is grocery delivery inside the Bolt Food app: a "supermarket in your pocket" with 3,500+ products (fresh produce, pantry staples, household essentials). Orders can be delivered to the door within about 30 minutes. No separate app â€” order from Bolt Food.

## Main Features
- **Fast delivery**: Shelf to doorstep in ~30 minutes (location-dependent)
- **3,500+ products**: Fresh produce, pantry, household essentials, vegan, ready-to-eat, pet food, etc.
- **Single app**: Order via Bolt Food; choose a Bolt Market store near you, add to basket, confirm and pay, track to door
- **Same-day**: Same-day delivery; instant delivery in as little as 30 minutes where available
- **Tracking**: Real-time driver tracking in Bolt Food app

## Problem Solved
Quick grocery runs without leaving home or downloading another app; same convenience as Bolt Food for meals, extended to full grocery shopping.

## Value Proposition
"Groceries delivered in minutes." One app for food and groceries; fast, convenient, easy ordering.

## Target Customers
Consumers who want fast grocery delivery; existing Bolt Food users who also need groceries.

## Pricing Model
Products priced in app; delivery and any small-order fees shown before confirm. Payment as for Bolt Food (cash, card, Bolt balance).
