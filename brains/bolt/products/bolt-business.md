# Bolt Business

## Company
Bolt

## Overview
Bolt Business is the B2B platform for managing company travel: control, monitor, and manage business rides (and related services) in one place. Used by 50,000+ businesses. Cuts travel costs, streamlines expenses, and simplifies payments with centralised accounts and invoicing. Bolt Business rides (Business Accounts) are certified CarbonNeutral®.

## Main Features
- **One platform**: Work rides (office, clients, airport), Bolt Drive car rental, e-scooters and e-bikes, Bolt Food for Business — all in one account
- **Save time**: Automated ride reports and third-party integrations; reduce manual expenses
- **Cut costs**: Competitive pricing; identify cost-saving opportunities without compromising quality
- **Control and visibility**: Custom spending limits and rules; real-time updates when team travels
- **Simple invoicing**: Single consolidated invoice for all team rides; no activation cost or minimum commitment
- **Setup**: Enter business details and payment method; add team members and optional spend/usage limits; organise into groups
- **Support**: Dedicated customer support in multiple languages; Help Centre

## Problem Solved
Scattered business travel, manual expense handling, lack of visibility and control over ride spend. Bolt Business centralises booking, spend limits, and reporting.

## Value Proposition
"Keeping your business moving." One platform for better business travel: save time, cut costs, full control and visibility, simple invoicing. CarbonNeutral® for business rides and micromobility.

## Target Customers
Companies of any size that need to manage team travel: work rides, client meetings, office commutes, airport trips; businesses that want to simplify expenses and reduce admin.

## Pricing Model
Pay per ride/service; competitive business pricing. No activation costs or minimum commitment. Invoicing and payment methods as per signup.
