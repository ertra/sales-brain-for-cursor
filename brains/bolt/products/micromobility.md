# Micromobility (Scooters & E-Bikes)

## Company
Bolt

## Overview
Bolt’s micromobility offers electric scooter and e-bike rental in the Bolt app: find a vehicle on the map, unlock with the app, pay as you go or via passes. Built for safety and sustainability; supports "cities for people, not cars" with less congestion and emissions. Scooters and e-bikes are certified CarbonNeutral®.

## Main Features
- **Scooters**: Bolt 6 (width, suspension, sensors, ~90 km range, 8-year lifespan); pay per minute or unlimited-unlocks / passes; reserve up to 30 minutes; beginner mode (15 km/h until 5 rides)
- **E-bikes**: Quick, convenient; recyclable materials; 25 km/h limit; helmet recommended; Safety Toolkit
- **Pricing options**: Pay as you go (from ~€0.15/min); unlimited unlocks (from ~€0.99/month); passes (set minutes)
- **Safety**: Beginner mode; tandem detection; cognitive reaction test at peak hours (ride sober); helmets encouraged; report badly parked scooters; AI pavement detection and parking guidance
- **Charging docks**: Bolt docks hold up to 10 units; compatible with all brands; reduce van/bike battery swaps (e.g. 50–60 fewer ops/week in Tallinn); better parking
- **City impact**: 12% of short car trips replaced by zero-emission scooter trips (claimed); 99.997% of scooter rides without safety incidents (2022)

## Problem Solved
Short urban trips without a car: faster commutes, lower cost, less traffic and pollution. Alternative to driving for first/last mile and local errands.

## Value Proposition
"Get there on a scooter" / "Not every day is a car day." Ride more, spend less; safe, sustainable, integrated in one app with rides, Drive, and food.

## Target Customers
Urban riders for short trips; commuters; anyone avoiding car use for environmental or cost reasons. Cities and municipalities (Bolt shares data and best practices).

## Pricing Model
Unlock fee + per-minute rate (varies by location); weekly/monthly passes for frequent riders. Pricing visible in app before ride.
