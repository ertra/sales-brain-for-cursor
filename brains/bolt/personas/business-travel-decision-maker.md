# Business Travel Decision-Maker

## Role/Title
Travel Manager, Office Manager, Head of Operations, Finance/Expenses Lead, HR (employee perks), Admin lead. Person who controls or influences how the company handles work travel and related spend.

## Responsibilities
- Control, monitor, and manage business travel for the organisation (or department)
- Set and enforce spending limits and booking rules; ensure visibility into who travels and how much is spent
- Reduce admin: streamline expense reporting, integrate with existing tools where possible
- Balance cost and convenience: cut travel costs without hurting employee experience (client meetings, office commutes, airport trips, team rides)
- Optionally own “office perks” (e.g. subsidised rides, Bolt Food for team)

## Company Profile
Any business using or considering Bolt Business — 50,000+ businesses across industries; from small teams to large enterprises. Use cases: work rides, client relations, commutes, airport transfers, Bolt Drive car rental, e-scooters/e-bikes, Bolt Food for Business.

## Key Problems & Pain Points
- Manual expense handling and scattered bookings; team using personal cards or multiple apps
- No single view of travel spend; hard to set limits or enforce policy
- Time spent on admin and reconciliation instead of higher-value work
- Want to cut costs but keep travel reliable and simple for employees

## Goals & Objectives
- One platform for work rides (and related services) with centralised billing and reporting
- Save time via automated ride reports and third-party integrations
- Cut travel costs with competitive pricing and clear cost-saving opportunities
- Full control and visibility: custom spending limits, rules, real-time updates when team travels
- Simple invoicing: one consolidated invoice, no activation cost or minimum commitment

## How Our Products Solve Their Problems
- **Bolt Business**: Single account for rides, Bolt Drive, e-scooters/e-bikes, Bolt Food for Business; custom spend and usage limits; groups for employees; real-time visibility; automated reports and integrations; single invoice; CarbonNeutral® for business rides (optional sustainability message).

## Buying Process
- Often owns or heavily influences travel and expense decisions; may involve Finance for invoicing and Procurement for larger deployments
- Values ease of setup (“no activation costs or minimum commitment”), integrations, and clear ROI (time saved, cost cut)
- Can start small (one team/group) and expand

## Communication Preferences
- Outcomes: save time, cut costs, full control, one invoice, less admin
- Proof: “50,000+ businesses,” automated reports, third-party integrations, competitive pricing
- Simplicity: one platform, quick to get started, no commitment
