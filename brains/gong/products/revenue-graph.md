# Gong Revenue Graph

## Company
Gong

## Overview
A living network of your revenue data that automatically captures and connects every customer interaction across your business. Turns raw interaction data into real insights by mapping it to business context.

## Main Features
- Automatic capture of every customer interaction (calls, meetings, emails, etc.)
- Mapping of interactions to business context (deals, accounts, contacts)
- Unified revenue data layer powering the rest of the Revenue AI OS
- Seamless knowledge transfer — complete customer context for every deal and account
- Foundation for AI agents and analytics across the platform

## Problem Solved
Revenue teams work with fragmented, manual data entry and scattered context. The Revenue Graph eliminates manual capture and gives a single source of truth so teams and AI can act on complete, connected revenue signals.

## Value Proposition
Ditch the manual work; get one rich, connected view of all revenue interactions. Enables smarter seller workflows, accurate forecasting, and AI that understands your actual customer conversations.

## Target Customers
Revenue teams (sales, CS, enablement, RevOps) and leaders who need a unified view of customer interactions and pipeline; organizations scaling GTM and wanting AI built on real revenue data.
