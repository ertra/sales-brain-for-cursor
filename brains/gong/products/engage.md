# Gong Engage

## Company
Gong

## Overview
Sales engagement and pipeline motion powered by revenue AI. Prioritize and personalize every sales engagement; send pipeline to the moon with AI keeping communications targeted and efficient. Retain and grow accounts with complete customer context and seamless handoffs.

## Main Features
- Smarter seller workflows for prospecting and outreach
- Personalization and prioritization of sales engagement
- Complete customer context (from Revenue Graph) in the flow of work
- Seamless handoffs between teams (e.g. sales to CS)
- Integration with pipeline and forecasting so engagement ties to outcomes
