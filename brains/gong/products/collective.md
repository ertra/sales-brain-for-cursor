# Gong Collective

## Company
Gong

## Overview
Curated integrations and services that help enterprises run revenue on Gong. Robust, out-of-the-box integrations and bespoke services so teams can connect Gong to their existing stack (CRM, communication, identity, productivity) and adopt revenue AI without replacing everything.

## Main Features
- 250+ integrations (e.g. Salesforce, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Slack, Okta, Microsoft 365 Copilot)
- Bi-directional sync with CRM and other systems for consistent business context
- Industry and use-case specific options (e.g. healthcare)
- Trust and security aligned with enterprise requirements
- Single platform for pipeline, forecasting, engagement, and insights

## Problem Solved
Revenue tools often sit in silos; data is duplicated and inconsistent. The Collective lets organizations consolidate onto Gong while keeping their preferred CRM, meetings, and collaboration tools.

## Value Proposition
Seamless revenue transformation with out-of-the-box integrations and one platform for data, intelligence, and workflows. Reduces integration burden and helps “any enterprise win more with Gong.”

## Target Customers
RevOps, IT, and revenue leaders evaluating or rolling out Gong; mid-market and enterprise accounts that need CRM, meetings, and identity integrations; industries with compliance needs (e.g. healthcare, financial services).
