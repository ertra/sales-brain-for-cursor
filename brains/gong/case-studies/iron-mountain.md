# Case Study: Iron Mountain

## Snapshot

## Executive Summary
Iron Mountain cut onboarding times in half using Gong. They measure ramp based on time to learn products, get in touch with customers, and start hitting quota. Gong enables faster onboarding and measurable impact for enablement and sales leadership.

## The Challenge

### Situation Before
- Onboarding and ramp took too long; need to get new hires productive faster
- Enablement needed to prove impact (time to productivity, not just completion)

### Key Pain Points
- Long time to productivity for new reps
- Need to measure ramp in business terms (learn, engage customers, hit number)
- Enablement under pressure to show ROI

## The Solution

### Why They Chose Gong
- Onboarding and ramp built from real customer conversations and context
- Visibility into execution so enablement and managers can coach and measure
- Clear metric: time to learn, engage customers, and hit number

### Products Implemented
| Product | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| Gong platform (Revenue Graph, visibility) | Onboarding; ramp; execution and adoption |
| Manager and enablement visibility | Coach where it counts; measure ramp |

## The Results

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Onboarding / ramp time | Baseline | Measured, reduced | Cut in half |
| Measure of ramp | Completion only | Learn → engage → hit number | Business outcome |

### Headline Results
- **Onboarding times cut in half** with Gong
- **Ramp measured** on learning products, engaging customers, and hitting number
- **Enablement impact** demonstrated in time-to-productivity

### Qualitative Benefits
- New hires get up to speed faster
- Enablement and sales leadership can prove impact with clear metrics
- Managers have visibility to coach during ramp
