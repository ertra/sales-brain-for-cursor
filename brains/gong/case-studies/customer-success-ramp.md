## Executive Summary
With Gong, CSM onboarding time was reduced from six months to just over two months. New CSMs get full account and customer context in one place, so they ramp faster and managers can coach from reality. Director of Global Customer Success & Programs Operations cited Gong as the key enabler for shrinking onboarding from six months to ~2 months.

## The Challenge

### Situation Before
- New CSMs took ~6 months to onboard and feel fully productive
- Context was scattered; knowledge in people's heads or across tools
- Ramp was a bottleneck for scaling CS

### Key Pain Points
- Long time to productivity for new CSMs
- Chasing context across systems
- Inconsistent coverage during turnover or growth

## The Solution

### Why They Chose Gong
- Centralized account and customer context in one platform
- New CSMs use Gong for full visibility without digging
- Managers coach from real data; less admin for CSMs

### Products Implemented
| Product | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| Gong Revenue Graph, platform | Full account context; post-sale visibility |
| Visibility for CSMs and managers | Ramp; coaching; "know where you stand" |

## The Results

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| CSM onboarding time | ~6 months | ~2 months | Down to just over 2 months |
| Time to full productivity | 6 months | ~2 months | Significant reduction |

### Headline Results
- **Onboarding time reduced from six months to just over two months** with Gong
- **New CSMs** get full context and ramp faster
- **Managers** can coach and support from reality, not hearsay

### Qualitative Benefits
- "My time to onboard began to significantly decrease"
- Onboarding time "shrinking from six months to five to four" and ultimately to just over two months
- CSMs and managers aligned on same context

## Customer Quotes

Ultimately today, I'm able to onboard a brand new CSM within just over two months down from six months pre-Gong."
