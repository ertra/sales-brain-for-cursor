## Executive Summary
Frontify consolidated pipeline management and forecasting onto Gong, aligning RevOps and reps on one platform. The main driver was consolidation — "one platform where you can do everything" — which led to a 30% increase in lead conversion and productivity with sophisticated insights.

## The Challenge

### Situation Before
- Multiple tools for pipeline, forecasting, and insights; RevOps and reps not aligned on the same data
- Need for productivity and sophisticated insights without stitching point solutions

### Key Pain Points
- Fragmented data and workflows
- RevOps and field not on the same page
- Lack of one place for pipeline management and forecasting

### Business Impact
- Missed conversion and alignment opportunities; reactive vs. strategic RevOps

## The Solution

### Why They Chose Gong
- Consolidation: one platform for pipeline management, forecasting, and insights
- Alignment between RevOps and reps on the same data
- Productivity and sophisticated insights in one place

### Products Implemented
