## Executive Summary
Uber for Business saved 6,700 hours across call prep, follow-up, and CRM updates using Gong. The AI Tracker agent helped lift buyer response rates by 32% by identifying which value props resonate most with different personas — demonstrating productivity and engagement impact at scale.

## The Challenge

### Situation Before
- Significant time spent on call prep, follow-up, and CRM updates
- Need to improve buyer response and pipeline; identify what resonates with different personas

### Key Pain Points
- Admin and manual work consuming rep and team time
- Generic or suboptimal messaging; unclear which value props work for which buyers
- Need for scale without proportional headcount

## The Solution

### Why They Chose Gong
- Revenue Graph and AI to automate capture and insights
- Gong AI Tracker agent to identify which value props resonate with different personas
- Automation of follow-ups and pipeline updates

### Products Implemented
| Product | Use Case |
|---------|----------|
| Gong Revenue Graph | Auto-capture; full context; data for AI |
| Gong AI Tracker agent | Identify value props that resonate by persona; improve response rates |
| Platform (Agents, workflows) | Call prep, follow-up, CRM updates automated |

## The Results

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Hours (prep, follow-up, CRM) | High manual | Automated / reduced | 6,700 hours saved |
| Buyer response rates | Baseline | AI-informed targeting | 32% lift |
| Value prop relevance | Generic | Persona-specific | Identified what resonates |

### Headline Results
- **6,700 hours saved** across call prep, follow-up, and CRM updates
- **32% lift in buyer response rates** with AI Tracker identifying value props by persona
- **Productivity at scale** without proportional headcount

### Qualitative Benefits
