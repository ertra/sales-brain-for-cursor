# Gong Revenue AI OS → Platform Value Proposition

## Summary
Gong is the #1 AI operating system for revenue teams. It unifies customer insights with the Gong Revenue Graph, delivers actionable intelligence through AI, and automates workflows so sales, CS, enablement, and RevOps work in sync. The result: productivity, predictability, and growth — with one source of truth and one platform from prospecting to expansion.

## The Problem
Revenue teams are stuck with fragmented data, manual work, and siloed tools. Forecasts are wrong, pipeline visibility is inconsistent, and winning behaviors don’t scale. Reps spend time on admin instead of selling; leaders lack unbiased visibility; and churn or risk surfaces too late.

## The Solution
One Revenue AI OS: the Revenue Graph captures every interaction and connects it to deals and accounts; purpose-built apps (Engage, Forecast, Agents, Collective) and AI agents turn that data into insights and actions. Every GTM team works from the same data and the same platform.
