# Persona: Sales Manager

## Role Overview
Sales Managers are frontline leaders responsible for coaching their team of sellers to hit quota. They balance rep development, deal reviews, forecasting, and their own leadership responsibilities. They need visibility into rep activities and deal health to coach effectively.

## Titles
- Sales Manager
- Regional Sales Manager
- Team Lead, Sales
- Sales Team Manager
- Inside Sales Manager
- SDR Manager
- Enterprise Sales Manager

## Reports To
- Director of Sales
- VP of Sales
- Sales Leader

## Key Responsibilities
- Coach and develop sales reps
- Conduct pipeline and deal reviews
- Submit accurate forecasts
- Hire and onboard new reps
- Monitor team activity and performance
- Help close strategic deals
- Implement sales process and methodology
- Hit team quota

## Goals & KPIs
| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Team Quota Attainment | % of team revenue target |
| Rep Productivity | Average quota attainment per rep |
| Ramp Time | Time for new reps to hit quota |
| Forecast Accuracy | Committed vs. actual |
| Coaching Hours | Time spent developing reps |
| Win Rate | Team average win rate |
| Attrition | Rep retention rate |

## Pain Points

### 1. No Time for Coaching
- Drowning in deal reviews and admin
- Firefighting vs. developing reps
- Don't know who needs help most
- Reactive coaching instead of proactive

### 2. Limited Visibility into Rep Performance
- Can't see what reps are doing
- Only know about problems when deals slip
- Hard to diagnose underperformance
- Incomplete CRM data

### 3. Pipeline Reviews Are Painful
- Hours spent in pipeline meetings
- Reps don't have accurate data
- Manual updating of spreadsheets
- Still get surprised by slippage

### 4. Forecasting is a Guessing Game
- Roll up rep forecasts blindly
- Don't trust the numbers
- Constantly adjusting commits
- Pressure from leadership

## Buying Triggers
- New to management role
- Team missing quota
- Scaling team (adding reps)
- High rep turnover
- Need to improve forecast accuracy
- Leadership mandate on coaching
