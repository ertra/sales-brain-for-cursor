# Persona: Revenue Operations

## Role Overview
Revenue Operations (RevOps) professionals are responsible for aligning sales, marketing, and customer success teams to drive predictable revenue growth. They own the tech stack, data integrity, process optimization, and reporting across the entire revenue engine.

## Titles
- VP of Revenue Operations
- Director of Revenue Operations
- Revenue Operations Manager
- Sales Operations Manager
- Business Operations Manager
- GTM Operations Lead

## Reports To
- Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)
- Chief Operating Officer (COO)
- VP of Sales

## Key Responsibilities
- Align sales, marketing, and CS teams for predictable revenue
- Manage and optimize the revenue tech stack
- Ensure data quality and CRM hygiene
- Build reporting and analytics infrastructure
- Design and implement sales processes
- Enable forecasting accuracy
- Drive operational efficiency across GTM teams

## Goals & KPIs
| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Tech Stack ROI | Return on revenue technology investments |
| Data Accuracy | CRM data quality and completeness |
| Forecast Accuracy | Variance between forecast and actual |
| Process Adoption | % of reps following defined processes |
| Time to Productivity | New rep ramp time |
| Cost per Opportunity | Efficiency of pipeline generation |

## Pain Points

### 1. Tech Stack Sprawl
- Too many disconnected point solutions
- High costs and low ROI on tools
- Integration challenges between systems
- Data silos across platforms

### 2. Data Quality Issues
- Incomplete CRM records
- Manual data entry errors
- Lack of activity capture
- Inconsistent processes

### 3. Limited Visibility
- Can't see what's working across teams
- Difficult to identify bottlenecks
- Reactive rather than proactive insights
- Forecast unpredictability

### 4. Change Management
- Low adoption of new tools and processes
- Reps resist workflow changes
- Hard to enforce best practices
- Training and enablement overhead

## Buying Triggers
- New CRO or VP Sales joining company
- Board pressure on revenue predictability
- Tech stack audit / consolidation initiative
- Series B+ funding requiring scale
- Missed forecast quarters
- High rep turnover
