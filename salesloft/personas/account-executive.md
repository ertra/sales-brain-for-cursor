# Persona: Account Executive

## Role Overview
Account Executives are quota-carrying sellers responsible for managing opportunities through the sales cycle and closing deals. They need to prioritize the right accounts, personalize engagement, progress deals efficiently, and hit their revenue targets.

## Titles
- Account Executive (AE)
- Senior Account Executive
- Enterprise Account Executive
- Strategic Account Executive
- Sales Executive
- Territory Account Manager
- Named Account Manager

## Reports To
- Sales Manager
- Director of Sales
- VP of Sales

## Key Responsibilities
- Manage and close sales opportunities
- Build relationships with prospects and customers
- Conduct discovery calls and product demos
- Navigate complex buying committees
- Negotiate contracts and pricing
- Hit quarterly and annual quota
- Maintain accurate pipeline and forecasts
- Collaborate with SDRs, SEs, and CS

## Goals & KPIs
| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Quota Attainment | % of revenue target achieved |
| Win Rate | % of opportunities closed won |
| Average Deal Size | Mean contract value |
| Sales Cycle Length | Days from opportunity to close |
| Pipeline Coverage | Open pipeline vs. remaining quota |
| Forecast Accuracy | Committed vs. actual closed |

## Pain Points

### 1. Too Many Accounts, Not Enough Time
- Don't know which deals to prioritize
- Spreading too thin across opportunities
- Missing signals that deals are at risk
- Working deals that won't close

### 2. Lack of Buyer Context
- Hours spent researching before calls
- Don't know who's engaged in deal
- Missing key stakeholders
- Flying blind into meetings

### 3. Deals Stall or Slip
- Losing momentum after demos
- Objections not handled well
- Decision makers go dark
- Forecast commits become misses

### 4. Administrative Burden
- Manual CRM updates
- Notes scattered across tools
- Prep time for every meeting
- Follow-up email writing

## Buying Triggers
