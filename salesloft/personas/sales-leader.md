# Persona: Sales Leader / VP of Sales

## Role Overview
Sales Leaders are responsible for driving revenue through their sales teams. They focus on hitting targets, coaching reps to success, and building predictable, scalable revenue engines. They need visibility into team performance, deal health, and accurate forecasting.

## Titles
- VP of Sales
- Senior Vice President of Sales
- Director of Sales
- Head of Sales
- Regional Sales Director
- Sales Director

## Reports To
- Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)
- CEO
- President

## Key Responsibilities
- Hit quarterly and annual revenue targets
- Build, manage, and coach sales teams
- Forecast revenue accurately to leadership/board
- Develop and execute sales strategy
- Hire and retain top sales talent
- Identify and replicate winning behaviors
- Manage pipeline health and deal progression

## Goals & KPIs
| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Revenue Attainment | % of quota achieved |
| Win Rate | % of opportunities closed won |
| Average Deal Size | Mean contract value |

| Pipeline Coverage | Pipeline vs. quota ratio |
| Forecast Accuracy | Predicted vs. actual results |
| Rep Productivity | Revenue per rep |

## Pain Points

### 1. Forecast Unpredictability
- Can't confidently call the number
- Deals slip unexpectedly
- Limited visibility into deal health
- Pressure from board/executives

### 2. Coaching at Scale
- Don't have time to coach every rep
- Hard to identify who needs help
- Can't replicate top performer behaviors
- Coaching deprioritized for firefighting

### 3. Rep Performance Variability
- Big gap between top and bottom performers
- Inconsistent execution of sales process
- Difficulty diagnosing performance issues
- New reps take too long to ramp

### 4. Pipeline Visibility
- Don't know which deals are truly at risk
- Incomplete data in CRM
- Reviews are time-consuming
- Reactive to problems

## Buying Triggers
- Missed forecast / revenue targets
- New to role (first 90 days)
- Scaling sales team significantly
- Board/investor pressure on growth
- High rep turnover
- Launching new product/market
