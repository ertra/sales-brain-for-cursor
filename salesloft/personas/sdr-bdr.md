# Persona: SDR / BDR (Sales Development Representative)

## Role Overview
SDRs and BDRs are responsible for generating qualified pipeline through outbound prospecting and inbound lead qualification. They are measured on meetings booked, opportunities created, and pipeline generated for Account Executives.

## Titles
- Sales Development Representative (SDR)
- Business Development Representative (BDR)
- Outbound Sales Representative
- Lead Development Representative
- Inside Sales Representative
- Market Development Representative

## Reports To
- SDR Manager
- Director of Sales Development
- VP of Sales

## Key Responsibilities
- Prospect and identify potential customers
- Execute multi-channel outreach (email, phone, social)
- Qualify inbound leads
- Book meetings for Account Executives
- Research accounts and contacts
- Maintain CRM data accuracy
- Hit activity and pipeline targets

## Goals & KPIs
| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Meetings Booked | Qualified meetings scheduled |
| Opportunities Created | Qualified opportunities generated |
| Pipeline Generated | Dollar value of pipeline created |
| Activities | Calls, emails, social touches |
| Conversion Rate | Leads to meetings/opportunities |
| Response Rate | % of outreach getting replies |

## Pain Points

### 1. Research Takes Too Long
- Hours spent researching each account
- Manual gathering of contact information
- Hard to find relevant personalization hooks
- Time not spent selling

### 2. Standing Out in Crowded Inbox
- Prospects get 100+ emails daily
- Generic outreach gets ignored
- Hard to personalize at scale
- Low response rates

### 3. Knowing Who to Prioritize
- Too many leads, not enough time
- Don't know which accounts are hot
- Guessing on who will respond
- Wasting time on wrong prospects

### 4. Repetitive Manual Work
- Copy/paste from multiple tools
- Manual activity logging
- Switching between applications
- Administrative busywork

## Buying Triggers

- High SDR turnover
- New outbound motion launch

- Current tool not driving results
