## Qualification Criteria

### Must-Haves
- [ ] B2B sales motion
- [ ] Dedicated sales team (5+ sellers minimum)
- [ ] CRM in place (Salesforce, HubSpot, Dynamics)
- [ ] Budget for sales technology
- [ ] Revenue growth objectives

### Nice-to-Haves
- [ ] Multiple sales segments (SDR, AE, CS)
- [ ] Complex/longer sales cycles
- [ ] Multi-channel outreach needs
- [ ] Forecasting challenges
- [ ] Sales coaching initiatives
- [ ] Tech stack consolidation goals

### Disqualifiers
- B2C or transactional sales only
- No dedicated sales team
- No CRM system
- Very early stage (<$1M ARR)
- Single-person sales "team"
