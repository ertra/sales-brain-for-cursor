# Deals

## Product Category
Deal Management / Opportunity Management

## Overview
Salesloft Deals gives sales teams the clarity and focus needed to win. AI agents help understand where every deal stands, what's at risk, and what needs to happen to speed up deal cycles and close more business.

## Tagline
"Manage Sales Opportunities & Close With Confidence"

## Key Features

### Close Focus Zone
- Prioritizes deals based on likelihood to close
- Helps reps work deals most likely to get them to quota
- Account planning agents rank opportunities
- Focus on end-of-quarter critical deals

### Auto Buying Group Capture
- Automatically creates CRM records for email/meeting recipients
- Associates contacts with correct deals
- Ensures sellers know who's in buying group
- No blockers go unnoticed

### Deal Qualification Frameworks
- Sales Methodology Extraction AI Agent
- Supports MEDDPICC, BANT, SPIN frameworks
- Pulls critical information from buyer conversations
- Drives predictable, scalable sales process

### Deal Analytics Overview Dashboard
- Comprehensive pipeline view with drill-down
- Identify and close deal gaps
- AI-recommended next steps
- Nothing hinders deal progress

### Deals Flow Chart
- Visualizes pipeline performance
- Shows percentage of deals converted to closed revenue
- Tracks idle deals and lost opportunities
- Informs forecasts and strategy
