# Conversations

## Product Category
Conversation Intelligence / Sales Coaching

## Overview
Salesloft Conversations is conversation intelligence software built into the all-in-one platform. AI agents help teams understand the impact of every sales conversation, share insights, and replicate the behaviors of top sellers through coaching and analysis.

## Tagline
"Understand Your Buyers and Team Performance"

## Key Features

### AI-Assisted Scorecards
- Pre-populates manager scorecards with AI assessments
- Analyzes conversation transcripts for rep performance
- Creates significant time-savings for managers
- Scorecards surface as action items in Rhythm workflow

### Summaries + Action Items
- Automatically generates conversation summaries
- Extracts action items from calls
- Helps sellers capture key details quickly
- Assists with follow-up email writing

### Conversation Key Moments
- Surfaces competitors, objections, and decision-makers
- Reviews transcripts for deal-impacting insights
- Identifies blockers before they become deal risks
- Helps prioritize actions affecting deal health

### Tracker Trends
- Visualizes patterns across multiple conversations
- Tracks competitor mentions and price sensitivity
- Identifies areas of opportunity
- Enables data-driven action

### Call Recording & Transcription
- Automatic recording and transcription
- No note-taking during calls required
- Easy reference for later review
- Full engagement in conversations

## Problems Solved

| Challenge | Solution |
