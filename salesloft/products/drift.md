# Drift

## Product Category
Conversational AI / Website Chat / Lead Generation

## Overview
Drift (acquired by Salesloft) is an AI chat agent that engages website visitors with real-time personalized conversations, delivering a better buyer experience and driving more qualified pipeline faster. It brings deeper insights to marketers and earlier buyer engagement to sellers.

## Tagline
"Convert Website Visitors Into Pipeline"

## Key Features

### AI Chat Agents
- Provides accurate and instant answers to visitor questions
- Personalized AI conversations keep prospects engaged
- Reduces drop-off and accelerates sales cycles
- Instantly qualifies leads

### Live Chat
- Skip forms and engage buyers in real-time
- Move prospects through funnel faster
- Direct connection to sales reps
- Personalized interactions

### Visitor Deanonymization
- Identifies high-intent buyers on website
- Captures relevant account data (company, location, history)
- Scores engagement in real-time
- Delivers right chat experience to convert

### Fastlane
- Uses tech stack data to identify and qualify high-value buyers
- Allows qualified buyers to skip form queue
- Connect immediately with sales
- Real-time qualification

### ROI Reporting
- Track engagement metrics
- Identify high-converting conversations
- Attribute success to pipeline and revenue
- Set strategic benchmarks

## Problems Solved

| Challenge | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Unmet buyer expectations | AI chat agents provide instant, accurate answers 24/7 |
| Lack of visibility into intent-based web traffic | Deanonymizes site visitors, identifies high-intent accounts |
| Lengthy and disconnected sales cycles | Routes qualified buyers into seller's Rhythm workflow |
| Not knowing which chats convert | ROI reporting tracks and attributes conversions |
