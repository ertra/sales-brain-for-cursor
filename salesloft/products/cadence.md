# Cadence

## Product Category
Sales Engagement / Pipeline Generation

## Overview
Salesloft Cadence is sales automation software that takes the guesswork out of pipeline generation, lead nurture, and renewals. It orchestrates every call, email, and meeting effortlessly while AI helps prioritize the right buyers and provides valuable insights to improve every interaction.

## Tagline
"Build and Nurture Pipeline"

## Key Features

### Account Agents
- Turn hours of work into minutes with AI agents
- Provides comprehensive account research
- Identifies buyer leads automatically
- Drafts personalized emails instantly

### Cadence Focus Zone
- AI-powered prioritization brings most engaged contacts to top
- Power through prospecting with flexibility
- Sort steps by channel, time zone, and account

### Dialer/Messenger
- Call buyers directly from platform, cell phone, or hard-lined phone
- Captures all important details automatically
- Works seamlessly with third-party dialers
- Text messaging via Messenger to boost engagement

### Calendaring (Salesloft Meetings)
- Integrates calendar with outbound communications
- Recipients can schedule time directly from emails
- Eliminates endless email exchanges

### Templates/Snippets
- Reusable, customizable messaging templates
- Quickly insert frequently used content
- Industry-specific details and FAQ answers
