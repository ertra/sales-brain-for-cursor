# Forecast

## Product Category
Sales Forecasting / Revenue Intelligence

## Overview
Salesloft Forecast transforms sales forecasting from a burdensome task into a strategic action plan to close more revenue. It combines real-time deal data and AI agents to improve forecast accuracy, bringing forecasting directly into the sales workflow.

## Tagline
"Confidently Call Your Number"

## Key Features

### AI Forecast Agent
- Predicts probability of deal being won in current quarter
- Trained on company's historical data
- Accounts for deal size, stage, and velocity
- Provides personalized benchmark to validate forecasts

### Forecast Segmentation
- Filter by new bookings vs. renewals
- Segment by business unit, region, account size
- Time period filtering (month/quarter)
- Flexible, customized forecasting

### Streamlined Rollups
- Visual representation of team progress toward goals
- Consolidates individual and team-level forecasts
- Complete view of team performance
- Identify gaps early

### Weekly Trend Chart
- Week-over-week view of pipeline health
- Tracks how forecast changes over time
- Combines data across forecast triangulations
- Reflects opportunity changes
