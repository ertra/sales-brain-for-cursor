# Rhythm

## Product Category
Sales Workflow Automation / Action Prioritization

## Overview
Salesloft Rhythm gives revenue teams the clarity they need to act first on what matters most. Rhythm uses AI to prioritize the most impactful seller actions, translating buyer signals into a prioritized workflow so sellers know exactly where to focus to win more deals.

## Tagline
"Take the Right Actions at the Right Moments"

## Key Features

### Priority Accounts Agent
- Ranks entire accounts based on engagement signals and buying intent
- Helps teams focus on accounts most likely to convert
- Enables true account-based strategy
- Seller-focused workflow agent

### Signals
- Unifies buying signals from across the platform and tech stack
- Captures proprietary data and custom sources
- Integrates with business intelligence apps
- Creates holistic view of buyer's digital footprint

### Plays
- Automations that prompt user actions
- Triggered by events and signals
- Actions include: meeting prep, agenda sharing, follow-up emails
- Triggers include: buyer intent scores, website engagement, customer health scores

### Focus Zones
- Run through prioritized list of actions by area of focus
- Cadence Focus Zone for pipeline generation
- Close Focus Zone for deals most likely to close
- Aligns seller's time with top priorities
