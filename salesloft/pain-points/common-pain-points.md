## Pain Point Discovery Questions

### For CRO/VP Sales
1. "How confident are you in your forecast right now, on a scale of 1-10?"
2. "What percentage of deals that were committed last quarter actually closed?"
3. "How aligned are your sales, marketing, and CS teams on a typical deal?"

### For Sales Managers
1. "How many hours per week do you spend in pipeline reviews?"
2. "How do you identify which reps need coaching before deals slip?"
3. "What's your new rep ramp time compared to where you'd like it?"

### For RevOps
1. "How many different tools does your sales team use daily?"
2. "What's your confidence level in your CRM data accuracy?"
3. "How long does it take to onboard a new tool or process?"

### For SDRs/AEs
1. "How much time do you spend researching before reaching out?"
2. "How do you decide which accounts to focus on each day?"
3. "What's your biggest time sink that isn't actually selling?"
