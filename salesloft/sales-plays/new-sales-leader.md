# Sales Play: New Sales Leader

## Play Overview
This play targets organizations that have recently hired a new CRO, VP of Sales, or Head of Sales. New leaders typically make technology and process changes in their first 90 days, making this a prime buying window.

## Trigger Signals
- LinkedIn announcement of new sales leadership hire
- Press release about new executive
- Job posting for the role (predecessor left)
- Board/investor announcement of leadership change
- Company growth news (often accompanies new leadership)

## Target Personas
**Primary**: New CRO, VP Sales, Head of Sales
**Secondary**: RevOps (often tasked with evaluating tools)

## Timing
- **Day 1-30**: Leader assessing current state
- **Day 31-60**: Planning changes and initiatives
- **Day 61-90**: Implementing first changes
- **Sweet spot**: Day 15-45 (assessing but not yet committed)

## Key Messages

### For the New Leader
"Congratulations on the new role. The first 90 days are critical for setting the foundation. Leaders who implement a unified revenue platform early see faster results because they have visibility from day one. What's your biggest priority right now?"

### Value Proposition
- **Immediate visibility** into team performance and pipeline
- **Scalable process** that can grow with your plans
- **Data foundation** for making informed decisions
- **Quick wins** with fastest time to value in industry

## Discovery Framework

### Understanding Their Mandate
1. "What brought you to this role? What's the board/CEO expecting?"
2. "What's the current state of the sales organization?"
3. "What tools and processes are in place today?"
4. "What are your first 90-day priorities?"

### Identifying Pain Points
1. "Do you have visibility into pipeline and forecast accuracy?"
2. "How standardized is the sales process across the team?"
3. "What's working that you want to keep?"
4. "What needs to change?"

### Aligning to Their Goals
1. "What does success look like in year one?"
2. "How will you measure if you're on track?"
3. "What would help you hit the ground running faster?"
