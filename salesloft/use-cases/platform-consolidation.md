# Use Case: Tech Stack Consolidation

## Overview
Organizations replace multiple disconnected point solutions with Salesloft's unified Revenue Orchestration Platform to reduce costs, improve data quality, and increase seller productivity.

## The Problem

### Current State
- 5-10+ different sales tools in use
- High total cost of ownership
- Data fragmented across systems
- Integration maintenance burden
- Low tool adoption due to complexity
- Reps waste time switching contexts

### Business Impact
- $200K-500K+ annual tool spend
- 20-30% of rep time on admin/tool switching
- Inaccurate data and forecasting
- Security and compliance complexity
- Slow onboarding for new reps
